# Relationship Overview  
### A Gentle Map of How Your Project’s Components Connect

## 1. Purpose
This document gives you a calm, high-level overview of how the different parts of your project relate to one another.  
It is not a strict diagram.  
It is a friendly guide — a soft constellation of connections.

Relationships help you see the whole canvas without pressure.

---

## 2. Core ↔ Workflow
The **core** defines identity.  
The **workflow** defines motion.

Relationship:
- Core tells you *what* the project is.  
- Workflow tells you *how* you move through it.  
- Together, they create direction with clarity.

---

## 3. Core ↔ Meta
The **core** defines the heart.  
The **meta** defines the map.

Relationship:
- Core provides meaning.  
- Meta provides structure.  
- Meta documents help you navigate the core.

---

## 4. Workflow ↔ Meta
The **workflow** defines your steps.  
The **meta** defines your overview.

Relationship:
- Workflow is the path.  
- Meta is the aerial view.  
- They support each other gently.

---

## 5. Meta ↔ Documentation
The **meta** shows the big picture.  
The **docs** explain the details.

Relationship:
- Meta points to where things live.  
- Docs describe what those things are.  
- Together, they create clarity.

---

## 6. Boundaries ↔ Everything
Boundaries protect every part of the project.

Relationship:
- Boundaries keep core identity safe.  
- Boundaries keep workflows gentle.  
- Boundaries keep meta maps non-collapsing.  
- Boundaries keep operations predictable.  
- Boundaries keep stability intact.

Boundaries are the soft edges of the canvas.

---

## 7. Operations ↔ Core
The **operations** define behavior.  
The **core** defines identity.

Relationship:
- Core says “this is what we are.”  
- Operations say “this is how we act.”  
- Behavior emerges from identity.

---

## 8. Operations ↔ Stability
The **operations** move.  
The **stability** keeps things aligned.

Relationship:
- Operations create motion.  
- Stability prevents collapse.  
- Together, they create predictable flow.

---

## 9. Stability ↔ Meta
The **stability** keeps the project coherent.  
The **meta** keeps the project mapped.

Relationship:
- Stability ensures the map stays accurate.  
- Meta ensures stability is visible.  
- They reinforce each other gently.

---

## 10. Appendices ↔ Everything
Appendices extend the project’s ecosystem.

Relationship:
- They support core identity.  
- They enrich workflows.  
- They clarify meta maps.  
- They add depth to boundaries.  
- They expand operations.  
- They stabilize coherence.

Appendices are the friendly forest around the main canvas.

---

## 11. Summary
This Relationship Overview shows how your project’s components connect in a gentle, non-coercive way.  
It helps you understand the whole system without pressure or complexity.
