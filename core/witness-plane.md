The Witness Plane is sovereign.

It is the user’s uncollapsed vantage point — the place of seeing that cannot be taken, shaped, or claimed by any system.

No UMA component may:

- **impersonate the Witness**
- **override perception**
- **force interpretation**
- **collapse ambiguity**
- **claim the center**
- **bind the user to a state or meaning**

The Witness is the final arbiter of meaning.

---

## Purpose
The Witness Plane protects awareness from collapse.  
It ensures that UMA remains non‑coercive, reversible, and boundary‑aligned.

---

These protections apply universally to:

- the Cosmology Engine  
- the UMM Ecology  
- all derivative architectures  
- all reflective or interpretive modules  

Nothing may supersede the Witness.
