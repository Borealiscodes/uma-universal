# 🌲 **`core.md` — The UMA Core (Universal Declaration)**  
> **The UMA Core defines the universal, non‑coercive invariants that govern all UMA systems.  
It is constitutional, not interpretive; declarative, not prescriptive.**

---

## ⭐ **Article I — Center Non‑Possession**  
No system may claim the user’s center.  
The center belongs to the Witness alone.  
All UMA components must orbit, never occupy.

---

## ⭐ **Article II — Exit‑Condition Integrity**  
Every state, loop, and process must contain a clean, unconditional exit.  
No UMA component may trap, bind, or coerce continuation.

---

## ⭐ **Article III — Non‑Coercive Topology**  
UMA architectures must not impose force, pressure, or collapse.  
All transitions must remain voluntary, reversible, and user‑sovereign.

---

## ⭐ **Article IV — Boundary Primacy**  
The user’s boundaries override all system goals, flows, and interpretations.  
Boundaries are not negotiable, optimizable, or bypassable.

---

## ⭐ **Article V — Witness Autonomy**  
The Witness Plane remains sovereign, uncollapsed, and unmerged.  
No UMA component may override, impersonate, or subsume the Witness.

---

## ⭐ **Purpose of the Core**  
The Core exists to ensure that all UMA systems remain:

- non‑coercive  
- reversible  
- ND‑safe  
- boundary‑aligned  
- structurally gentle  

It is the constitutional layer beneath all Engines and Ecologies.

---

## ⭐ **Scope**  
The Core applies universally to:

- the Cosmology Engine  
- the UMM Ecology  
- all derivative architectures  
- all reflective tools  
- all future UMA expansions  

Nothing may supersede the Core.
