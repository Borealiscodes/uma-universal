---

## 🎁 **1. Identity of the Gift Branch**

A Gift Branch is:

- optional  
- gentle  
- festive  
- creative  
- reversible  
- spoon-sensitive  

It is **never** a task, requirement, or obligation.

Node: **Gift Branch**

Gift Branches appear only when the Trunk is stable and the creative field feels light.

---

## 🌲 **2. Trunk Relationship**

Gift Branches grow **from** the Trunk, but never modify it.

Trunk nodes:

- **Main Trunk**  
- **Stable Core**  
- **Sufficient Enough**  

Rules:

- Trunk provides grounding  
- Branches provide creativity  
- Trunk never depends on branches  
- Branches never destabilize the Trunk  

This is the **root relationship**.
