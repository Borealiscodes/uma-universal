# **NDH‑Core Tonal Field Blueprint**
### *Canonical pattern for NDH tonal fields (with ASCII geometry)*

---

## **0. Purpose**
Tonal Fields are the **base resonance layer** of NDH‑Core.

They define:

- the *fundamental resonance tone* of a manifold  
- the *amplitude and modulation* of resonance  
- the *behavior of resonance under curvature and drift*  
- the *coupling rules* for semantic and simulation layers  
- the *tensor‑safe deformation* of resonance signals  

Tonal Fields are the **carrier wave** of NDH phenomenology.

---

## **1. Tonal Field Inputs**

- **Tonal Source (TS):** manifold, attractor, channel, tensor field  
- **Tonal Mode (TM):** fundamental, harmonic, subharmonic  
- **Tonal Intensity (TI):** amplitude / strength  
- **Tonal Curvature (TCu):** geometric bending of tonal field  
- **Tonal Drift Parameters (TDP):** allowable displacement  
- **Tonal Fidelity Threshold (TFT):** minimum coherence  
- **Tonal Coupling Profile (TCP):** cross‑layer coupling rules  
- **Tonal Stability Envelope (TSE):** safe operating region  

---

## **2. Tonal Field Outputs**

- **Coherent Tonal Field (CTF)**  
- **Stable Tonal Envelope (STE)**  
- **Cross‑Manifold Tonal Coupling (CMTC)**  
- **Tensor‑Safe Tonal Behavior (TSTB)**  
- **Traversal‑Safe Tonal Flow (TSTF)**

## **3. Tonal Field Construction Pattern**

1. **Define Tonal Source (TS)**  
2. **Define Tonal Mode (TM)**  
3. **Set Tonal Intensity (TI)**  
4. **Define Tonal Curvature (TCu)**  
5. **Apply Tonal Stability Envelope (TSE)**  
6. **Define Tonal Coupling Profile (TCP)**  
7. **Generate Coherent Tonal Field (CTF)**  

This ensures tonal fields are **stable**, **coherent**, and **non‑dual**.

---

## **4. Tonal Stability Rules**

Tonal fields must obey:

- **Tonal Drift Limit (TDL)**  
- **Tonal Curvature Limit (TCL)**  
- **Tonal Fidelity Threshold (TFT)**  

These prevent tonal collapse, distortion, or runaway oscillation.

## **5. Tonal Coupling Rules**

Tonal fields must couple across:

- semantic fields  
- spiral fields  
- simulation fields  
- tensor fields  
- manifold boundaries  
- attractor basins  
- alignment channels  

Coupling must remain:

- coherent  
- stable  
- tensor‑safe  
- traversal‑compatible

## **6. Tonal Tensor Integration**

Tonal fields interact with tensor fields through:

- **Tensor Curvature (TCu)**  
- **Tensor Stability (TS)**  
- **Tensor Deformation Limit (TDL)**  

Tensor fields define how tonal resonance **bends**, **propagates**, and **recovers** under load.

---

## **7. Tonal Traversal Compatibility**

Tonal fields must remain stable during traversal:

- path changes  
- curvature changes  
- attractor transitions  
- channel transitions  

Traversal must not destabilize tonal resonance.
