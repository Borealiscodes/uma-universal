# **Outreach Packet Protocol v1.0**

**Module:** UMM‑OP‑P‑01

**Layer:** Governance → Safeguards → Final Safety Net  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Owner:** Borealis S. Hedling  
**Bound Systems:** UMA → UMM → Safeguards → Final Safety Net → SIAP

---

## **1. Purpose**

The Outreach Packet Protocol v1.0 establishes the **formal governance rules**, **isolation boundaries**, and **safety mechanisms** required for the Outreach Packet subsystem to operate safely within UMA.

This protocol ensures:

- strict separation of personal, governance, and academic context  
- no subsystem bleed  
- no traversal misbinding  
- no confusion activation  
- no narrative or identity drift  
- no HRD misrouting  
- no Play Engine contamination  

The Outreach Packet subsystem becomes a **controlled, non‑interactive, governance‑aligned artifact generator**.

---

## **2. Scope**

This protocol governs:

- Outreach Packet drafting  
- Outreach Packet structure  
- Outreach Packet metadata  
- Outreach Packet isolation  
- Outreach Packet activation rules  
- Outreach Packet safety rails  
- Outreach Packet integration with SIAP and Safeguards  

It does **not** govern:

- runtime conversation  
- identity formation  
- emotional context  
- legal interpretation  
- traversal behavior  

---

## **3. Subsystem Isolation Rules**

The Outreach Packet subsystem is isolated from:

- Play Engine  
- HRDProtectionLayer  
- CHS‑OL traversal  
- Companion Constellation  
- HBR  
- SIAP runtime hooks  

Isolation requirements:

- No emotional scaffolding  
- No legal context  
- No narrative bleed  
- No traversal triggers  
- No cross‑plane context  
- No runtime activation  

Any violation triggers automatic quarantine under the Final Safety Net.

## **4. Activation Rules**

The Outreach Packet subsystem may activate only when:

- Safeguards v1.0 is active  
- Final Safety Net v1.0 is active  
- Outreach Packet Protocol v1.0 is present  
- Outreach Packet Pin is active  
- SIAP verifies alignment  
- Developer Log records the activation  

Activation is **blocked** if:

- any subsystem shows confusion  
- traversal nodes are unsealed  
- HRD is in legal‑context mode  
- Play Engine is active  
- Companion Constellation is in runtime mode
