# **Phase‑12 Development Boundary (v1.0)**  
### *Haptic Comprehension • Sensory Safety • Dignity Constraints*  
### *A boundary that prevents drift, misuse, or misinterpretation.*

Phase‑12 is the **sensory accessibility layer** of NDH.  
This boundary defines what Phase‑12 *is*, what it *is not*, and what it *must never become*.

---

## **Phase‑12 Development Boundary**

### **1. Phase‑12 Is Haptic Comprehension, Not Immersion**
Phase‑12 provides **safe, bounded haptic feedback** that helps users *perceive* NDH geometry.

It is **not**:

- XR  
- VR  
- AR  
- MR  
- Unity  
- game engines  
- immersive simulation  

Phase‑12 is **comprehension**, not entertainment.

---

### **2. Phase‑12 Must Never Replicate Harm**
No Phase‑12 output may:

- simulate collapse  
- reenact trauma  
- induce panic  
- overwhelm sensory channels  
- mimic coercive environments  

Phase‑12 is **protective**, not extractive.

---

### **3. Phase‑12 Feedback Must Be Mathematically Bounded**
All haptic signals must be constrained by:

- holonomy curvature  
- NDA operator bounds  
- stability geometry envelopes  
- collapse‑resistance surfaces  

No unbounded feedback.  
No spikes.  
No runaway amplification.

---

### **4. Phase‑12 Must Preserve Dignity**
Every sensory output must satisfy:

> **Dignity is a non‑negotiable constraint.**

This prohibits:

- shock  
- fear induction  
- destabilizing pulses  
- sensory coercion  
- forced immersion  

Phase‑12 is **dignity‑aligned**.

---

### **5. Phase‑12 Must Be Accessible Across Cognitive Styles**
Phase‑12 must support:

- tactile learners  
- spatial learners  
- narrative learners  
- analytical learners  
- neurodivergent users  

This is **inclusion by design**, not optional.

---

### **6. Phase‑12 Must Not Require Technical Knowledge**
Users must be able to understand Phase‑12 output without:

- tensor calculus  
- manifold theory  
- holonomy math  
- NDA familiarity  

Phase‑12 is **interpretation**, not instruction.

---

### **7. Phase‑12 Must Not Override Cognitive Autonomy**
Phase‑12 cannot:

- direct decisions  
- manipulate behavior  
- induce emotional states  
- bias judgment  
- override agency  

It provides **information**, not influence.

---

### **8. Phase‑12 Must Be Compatible With Myth Engineering**
Phase‑12 haptics must integrate with:

- narrative comprehension  
- metaphorical framing  
- intuitive geometry  
- cultural accessibility  

Myth Engineering is the **cognitive wrapper** for Phase‑12.

---

### **9. Phase‑12 Must Be Safe Under Adversity**
Because NDH is built under extreme circumstances:

> **Phase‑12 must remain safe even when development conditions are hostile.**

This boundary prevents environmental drift.

---

### **10. Phase‑12 Must Be Forward‑Compatible**
Future NDH modules must inherit Phase‑12 constraints automatically.

No subsystem may bypass:

- dignity  
- boundedness  
- accessibility  
- non‑coercion  
- stability geometry  

Phase‑12 is a **permanent boundary**, not a temporary guideline.

---

## **One‑Sentence Boundary Summary**
> **Phase‑12 provides safe, bounded, dignity‑aligned haptic comprehension of NDH geometry, and must never drift into immersion, coercion, harm replication, or unbounded sensory output.**
