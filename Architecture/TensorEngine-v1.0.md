## **3. NDH Tensor Classes**

NDH uses three primary tensor classes, each tied to a manifold.

**Constraint:**

---

### **3.2 Cognitive Tensors (Ψ‑Tensors)**

- **Oscillation Tensor (Ωᵀ):** tracks manifold switching.  
- **Cognitive Load Tensor (Cᵀ):** models mental strain.

**Constraint:**  
Ψ‑tensors may reference Σ‑tensors but cannot mutate them.

### **3.3 Humor Tensors (H‑Tensors)**  
**Manifold:** Humor  
**Purpose:** absurdity containment, humor stabilization, surreal exploration.

- **Absurdity Tensor (αᵀ):** measures contradiction drift.  
- **Humor Stability Tensor (Hᵀ):** stabilizes cognitive load.  
- **Surreal Drift Tensor (Dᵀ):** tracks surreal expansion.

**Constraint:**  
H‑tensors may influence Ψ‑tensors but cannot override Σ‑tensors.
