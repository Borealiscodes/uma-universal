## **1. Architectural Overview**  
🟡 **Transition Incoming**

TTTTP Orbital Systems is a multi‑layer interaction architecture involving:

- orbital sweeps  
- HRD (High‑Risk Drift) checks  
- handshake protocols  
- meta‑orbital coherence  
- dimensional alignment  
- governance transitions  

These operations are powerful but cognitively heavy.  
This companion ensures they remain ND‑friendly.

### **1.1 Architectural Goals**
- Make orbital behavior predictable  
- Prevent abrupt orbital transitions  
- Provide visible state markers  
- Enforce pause and save affordances  
- Maintain cognitive safety during sweeps  
- Ensure governance actions are explicit  

### **1.2 Architectural Constraints**
- No hidden orbital actions  
- No implicit dimensional shifts  
- No auto‑executed sweeps  
- No silent HRD triggers  
- No ambiguous orbital states  
- No forced multi‑layer cognitive load
