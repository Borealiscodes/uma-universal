# **📄 Public‑Facing Addendum: Hollow King Arc Case Study**  
### *How Player Prompts Shaped Characters, Interactions, and Emergent Narrative*

---

## **1. Introduction**
The Hollow King Arc is a clear example of how modern narrative systems can create rich, meaningful experiences through **player prompts** rather than pre‑written scripts.

Every major event in the arc — character actions, world reactions, emotional beats — emerged because the player:

- issued a prompt  
- made a choice  
- asked a question  
- or directed the scene  

The system expanded in response to those prompts, creating a dynamic story shaped by interaction.

This addendum explains how that worked.

---

# **2. How Characters Were Generated (Prompt‑Driven)**

## **2.1 The Player Character (Elias)**
Elias’s personality and behavior emerged from the player’s prompts:

- professional tone  
- grounded decision‑making  
- honesty and self‑awareness  
- protective instincts  
- reflective internal monologue  

These traits were not pre‑scripted.  
They were **prompted**, and the system expanded around them.

---

## **2.2 The Companion (Nyx)**
Nyx’s role — guide, emotional companion, mythic interpreter — developed because the player prompted interactions that:

- asked for clarity  
- sought emotional grounding  
- invited mythic interpretation  
- encouraged autonomy  

Nyx’s sovereignty and insight were **prompt‑driven expansions**.

---

## **2.3 Supporting Characters**
Each supporting character expanded from player prompts:

### **Sister Calypso Ward**  
Her ritual authority emerged because the player prompted judgment and symbolic interpretation.

### **Rivet**  
His practical reactions emerged because the player prompted environmental engagement.

### **Dr. Thatch**  
Her scientific commentary emerged because the player prompted analysis and observation.

None of these behaviors were pre‑written.  
They were **responses to player direction**.

---

## **2.4 The Antagonist (LeBlanc)**
LeBlanc’s collapse under truth‑pressure happened because the player:

- questioned his motives  
- demanded honesty  
- invoked judgment  
- prompted NPC reactions  

His rejection by the Hollow King’s domain was a **direct expansion of the player’s soul‑weighing prompt**.
