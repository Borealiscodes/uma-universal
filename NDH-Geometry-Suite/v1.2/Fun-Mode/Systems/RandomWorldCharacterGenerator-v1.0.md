# **🟧 NDH Random World & Character Generator v1.0 — “Genesis Layer”**

---

# **🟦 1. Core Purpose**
The Genesis Layer creates **fully governed random worlds and characters** using:

- Genre selection  
- Play‑style selection  
- World logic (Fantasy / Historical / Hybrid)  
- Civ‑style terrain & yields  
- Magic‑system grammar  
- NPC emotional logic  
- Tactical systems  
- Easter Egg micro‑events  

It produces **rich world‑building**, not mobbing, not chaos.

---

# **🟧 2. World Generation Pipeline (WGP v1.0)**

### **Step 1 — Genre Roll**
Weighted table:

- Fantasy (35%)  
- Historical (25%)  
- Hybrid (20%)  
- Solarpunk (10%)  
- Surreal (10%)  

### **Step 2 — Play‑Style Roll**
- **Deep RP**  
- **Exploration**  
- **Tactical**  
- **Cinematic**  
- **Mystery**  

### **Step 3 — Terrain Roll (Civ‑style hex)**
- Plains  
- Hills  
- Forest  
- River  
- Mountain  
- Coast  
- Sacred Site  
- Mage Tower Hex  
- Industrial Zone  

### **Step 4 — World Yield Roll**
Each world receives:

- Food  
- Production  
- Culture  
- Science  
- Influence  
- Mana (if fantasy)  
- Arcana (if magic‑dense)  

### **Step 5 — Magic Density Roll**
- None  
- Low  
- Moderate  
- High  
- Mythic  

### **Step 6 — Tactical Density Roll**
- None  
- Skirmish  
- Formation  
- Army‑scale  
- Siege  

### **Step 7 — Easter Egg Roll**
- Lore  
- NPC  
- Mechanic  
- Dimensional  
- Emotional  
- Puzzle
