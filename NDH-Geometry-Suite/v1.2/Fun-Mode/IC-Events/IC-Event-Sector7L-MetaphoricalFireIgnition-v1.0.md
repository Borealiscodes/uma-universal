# **🟧 IC — Lira Quell Enters the Fire (Metaphorical)**  
### *Dynamic NPC world-building activated*

The Ministry atrium lights flicker — not the usual polite flicker, but the kind that suggests someone has accidentally plugged the sun into a bureaucratic coffee machine.

The solar‑printed floor warms under Lira’s boots.

A voice crackles through the atrium’s intercom, sounding like a cheerful receptionist who has just discovered a small apocalypse in the break room:

> **“Attention, Department of Improvised Futures:  
> A Category‑Orange Spontaneous Metaphorical Fire has been detected in Sector 7‑L.  
> All junior architects are encouraged to panic in an orderly fashion.”**

Lira freezes.

Her sun‑seed pulses bright gold.

The Ministry’s pneumatic mail tubes begin firing emergency memos like confetti cannons. One slams into her chest. She unfolds it.

**Form 88‑B‑Supplemental: Declaration of Metaphorical Combustion.**

The form is already filled out.

By someone who is not her.

And the signature line reads:

> **“Filed on your behalf,  
> The Narrative Hazard Containment Office.”**

The atrium doors hiss open.

A wave of shimmering heat rolls in — not real heat, but the kind of symbolic temperature spike that means the universe is trying to make a point.

The world reacts:

- The bioluminescent vines along the ceiling curl away from the metaphorical blaze.  
- The Ministry’s solar drones begin spraying “Conceptual Fire Suppressant Foam.”  
- A bureaucratic alarm chimes in a jaunty, upbeat melody that absolutely does not match the situation.  
- A holographic advisory panel materializes, displaying the message:  
  **“Sector 7‑L: Narrative Ignition Event in Progress.”**

Lira swallows hard.

Her coat pockets rustle — several of the items inside are trying to evacuate on their own.

She mutters:

> **“Right. Into the fire we go.”**

And steps forward.

The world shifts around her.

Sector 7‑L is already rebuilding itself.
