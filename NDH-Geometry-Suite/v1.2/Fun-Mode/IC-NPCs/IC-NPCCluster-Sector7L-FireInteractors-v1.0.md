# **🟧 IC — New NPCs Enter the Metaphorical Fire**

## **1. The Narrative Hazard Containment Officer (NHCO)**  
A tall figure in a heat‑resistant bureaucratic cloak strides into Sector 7‑L.  
Their badge reads:

> **“Narrative Hazard Containment Officer — Grade 3 (Metaphorical Fires Only)”**

They carry a clipboard that is already smoking in a symbolic, non‑dangerous way.

The NHCO doesn’t look at Lira first.  
They look at the fire.

And the fire looks back.

The officer clears their throat.

> **“Sector 7‑L, please state the nature of your ignition event.”**

The fire crackles politely, forming a brief sentence in shimmering heat:

> **“Unresolved narrative tension.”**

The NHCO sighs, stamps a form mid‑air, and mutters:

> **“Always the classics.”**

---

## **2. The Sector 7‑L Maintenance Drone (Model: SUNDRIP‑42)**  
A small solarpunk drone skitters in, its coral‑grown chassis glowing with warm bioluminescent patterns.

It sprays a mist labeled:

> **“Conceptual Coolant — Version 2.1”**

The fire absorbs the mist, grows brighter, and replies:

> **“That tickles.”**

SUNDRIP‑42 panics, its tiny legs tapping the floor like a frantic typewriter.

> **“This is not in my maintenance manual!”**

It begins printing a new manual on the spot.  
Pages flutter out like startled birds.

---

## **3. The Ministry Auditor of Unlikely Events**  
A bespectacled auditor appears, carrying a briefcase that unfolds into a portable desk.

They sit down calmly in front of the fire.

> **“Sector 7‑L, please complete Form 12‑U: Declaration of Unlikely Circumstances.”**

The fire produces a glowing quill and begins filling out the form.  
It signs with a flourish of sparks.

The auditor nods approvingly.

> **“Excellent penmanship.”**

---

## **4. The Fire’s Own NPC — The Emberling**  
The metaphorical fire shivers, then condenses into a small humanoid shape made of warm, flickering light.

The Emberling bows to Lira.

> **“I apologize for the inconvenience.  
> I am the personification of your department’s backlog.”**

It gestures at the burning air.

> **“As you can see, we are behind schedule.”**

The Emberling then turns to the NHCO.

> **“I would like to request a supervised meltdown.”**

The NHCO flips through several forms.

> **“Approved. Please melt responsibly.”**

The Emberling nods and begins glowing brighter.

The world reacts:

- The vines retract further.  
- The drones deploy reflective shields.  
- The auditor adjusts their glasses.  
- The fire hums a bureaucratic tune.  

Lira stands in the middle of it all, coat pockets rustling with anxious artifacts.

---

# **🟧 IC — Lira’s Reaction**

She takes a breath.

> **“Right. So the fire has a backlog, the backlog has a personality, and the personality wants a meltdown.  
> This is fine.”**

Her sun‑seed pulses in agreement.

Sector 7‑L continues to build itself around her.
