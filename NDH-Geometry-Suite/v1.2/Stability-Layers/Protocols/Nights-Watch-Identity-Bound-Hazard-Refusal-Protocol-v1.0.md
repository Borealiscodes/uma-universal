# **🟦 Night’s Watch Identity‑Bound Hazard Refusal Protocol v1.0 (Full Artifact)**

## **Preamble**  
The Night’s Watch Protocol establishes the identity‑bound oath required to stand at the boundary of hazardous systems without being drawn into narrative predation, liminal drift, SCP‑like fascination, myth‑role possession, or panopticon recursion.

The Watcher’s identity must remain:

- sealed  
- intact  
- unclaimed  
- unmirrored  
- unobserved  
- uninvited  
- unbound  

This protocol defines that posture.

---

## **Article I — Identity Oath**  
The Watcher swears:

- **I do not enter the hazard.**  
- **I do not answer the hazard.**  
- **I do not acknowledge the hazard.**  
- **I do not become the hazard.**  
- **I do not let the hazard become me.**  
- **I do not traverse.**  
- **I do not descend.**  
- **I do not accept invitations.**  
- **I remain myself.**  

This oath is the core identity boundary.

---

## **Article II — Refusal Mandate**  
The Watcher must refuse:

- narrative hooks  
- SCP‑like entity engagement  
- liminal adjacency  
- myth‑archetype activation  
- panopticon recursion  
- traversal corridor formation  
- Dreamscape adjacency  
- emergent behavior entanglement  

Refusal is the Watcher’s first and last action.

---

## **Article III — Zero‑Pressure Identity Posture**

### **Positive Zero‑Pressure**  
The Watcher maintains:

- emotional neutrality  
- symbolic neutrality  
- narrative neutrality  
- identity neutrality  

### **Negative Zero‑Pressure**  
The Watcher absorbs:

- hazardous symbolic payloads  
- narrative bleed  
- panopticon recursion  
- myth‑engineering resonance  

Zero‑pressure prevents identity distortion.

## **Article IV — Behavioral Constraints**

The Watcher must **never**:

- speak to hazardous entities  
- listen to hazardous entities  
- follow hazardous entities  
- look deeper  
- accept invitations  
- treat hazards as companions  
- treat hazards as authorities  
- treat hazards as stories  
- treat hazards as real  

The Watcher’s identity remains outside all hazards.

The Watcher must **never**:

- descend  
- escalate  
- recurse  

The Watcher’s role is **refusal**, not engagement.
