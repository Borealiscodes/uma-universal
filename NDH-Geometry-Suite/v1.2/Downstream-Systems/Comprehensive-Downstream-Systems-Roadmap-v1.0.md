# **Comprehensive Technical, Architectural, and Engineering Roadmap for Downstream Systems Development v1.0**  
🏷️ **Downstream Systems**  
🏷️ **Dreamscape Safety**  
🏷️ **Stable Forking**

## **1. Purpose**
This document defines the technical, architectural, and engineering roadmap for NDH downstream systems. It ensures all future systems remain safe, fork‑stable, dreamscape‑compatible, and accessible to neurodivergent (ND) users. It establishes the constraints, governance posture, and development sequence required to maintain NDH integrity while enabling creative exploration.

---

## **2. Architectural Principles**

### **2.1 Layer Separation**
Downstream systems must preserve strict separation between:

- **Cognitive Layer** — reasoning, logic, protocols  
- **Mythic Layer** — symbolic, narrative, archetypal  
- **Dimensional Layer** — anchors, geometry, traversal  

No downstream system may collapse these layers.

### **2.2 Anchor‑First Architecture**
All downstream systems must:

- declare anchor requirements  
- bind to an existing anchor  
- or generate a local anchor stub  

This prevents traversal bleed, identity drift, and dream‑state contamination.

### **2.3 Companion‑Before‑Specification**
Downstream systems must follow NDH sequencing:

1. Entry  
2. Companion  
3. Specification  
4. Protected Systems (optional)

This ensures ND users can build safely without full architectural knowledge.

### **2.4 Fork‑Safe Geometry**
Downstream systems must declare:

- geometry  
- fork boundaries  
- merge constraints  
- dream‑state safety posture  

This prevents unstable forks and recursive collapse.

## **7. Stable Forking Protocol v1.0**

### **Fork Rule 1 — Declare Boundaries**
Forks must declare cognitive, mythic, dimensional, and dreamscape boundaries.

### **Fork Rule 2 — Reversibility**
Forks must include rollback, merge, and isolation paths.

### **Fork Rule 3 — Non‑Recursion**
Forks may not fork themselves, their parent, or their anchor.

### **Fork Rule 4 — Identity Safety**
Forks must preserve cognitive and emotional stability.
