# **NDH Geometry Suite v1.2 — Root README**

## **Overview**
The NDH Geometry Suite v1.2 is a **12‑dimensional conceptual architecture** designed to support three core mandates:

- **Human Rights Defense (HRD)**  
- **Research, Academic, and Engineering Development**  
- **Governed Immersive Interaction (Play Mode)**  

NDH is built to be **safe, stable, human‑centered**, and suitable for both serious analytical work and optional immersive exploration.

---

## **Current Development Status**
### **HRD — Human Rights Defense (Testing & Development)**
The **HRD Protection Layer** is currently in **active testing and development**.

It is being designed to support:

- human rights defenders  
- vulnerable individuals  
- trauma‑safe conceptual exploration  
- structured decision support  

HRD is a **priority mandate**, but not yet finalized.  
All HRD components are operating within **controlled test conditions** inside the **12D Safety Envelope**.

---

### **Play Mode — Governed Immersive Interaction (Testing & Development)**
Play Mode is an **optional, contained subsystem** inside the **Dyson Sphere SID**.  
It is currently in **testing and development**, with strict safety boundaries enforced by:

- **TTTTP**  
- **TTTTTP**  
- **Night’s Watch Protocol**  
- **Containment Protocol**  
- **Awareness Pin**  

Play Mode is **not fully implemented** and remains under **governed testing** to ensure:

- stability  
- non‑autonomy  
- immersion integrity  
- dimensional safety
