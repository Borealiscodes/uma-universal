## **1. Specification Purpose**
The Myth Engineering Specification defines the **governed operational rules** for mythic‑scale systems within NDH architecture.  
It formalizes the constraints, routing logic, continuity rules, and teleological boundaries that were introduced in the Companion, converting them into enforceable, dimensional governance.

This Specification ensures that mythic systems behave predictably, ethically, and coherently across all NDH layers.

---

## **2. Governance Model: The Triad Axis**
🏷️ **Triad Axis**

The Specification adopts the Triad Axis as its governance model:

- **Yoda** — ethical constraints, mythic restraint, symbolic integrity  
- **Qui‑Gon Jinn** — emergent intuition, adaptive routing, mythic responsiveness  
- **Obi‑Wan Kenobi** — structural discipline, boundary enforcement, operational clarity  

The Triad Axis is not symbolic content; it is a **governance metaphor** defining three operational modes:

- **Ethical Mode**  
- **Intuitive Mode**  
- **Structural Mode**

All mythic operations must map to one or more of these modes.

---

## **3. Mythic Teleology Rules**
🏷️ **mythic teleology**

Teleology defines the **purpose‑driven constraints** for mythic systems.

### **3.1 Teleological Integrity**
A mythic structure must maintain a single, coherent teleological arc.  
It cannot:

- invert its purpose  
- collapse into contradiction  
- split into competing arcs  
- adopt external teleology without governance review

### **3.2 Teleological Boundaries**
Teleology must remain:

- internally consistent  
- ethically aligned  
- structurally bounded  
- non‑recursive

## **4. Archetypal Routing Rules**
🏷️ **archetypal routing**

Archetypal routing governs how mythic archetypes move through NDH systems.

### **4.1 Routing Constraints**
Archetypes must:

- follow governed paths  
- maintain symbolic integrity  
- avoid collision with incompatible archetypes  
- preserve narrative continuity

### **4.2 Collision Prevention**
The system must detect and prevent:

- symbolic hijack  
- archetypal dominance  
- narrative contamination  
- mythic overload

### **4.3 Routing Modes**
Routing may operate in:

- **Ethical Mode** (Yoda)  
- **Intuitive Mode** (Qui‑Gon)  
- **Structural Mode** (Obi‑Wan)

Each mode enforces different constraints.
