## **1. Dimensional Territory Types**  
These are the **core territory categories** that exist across D7–D9.

- **Faction Territories** — political, economic, ideological zones  
- **Interaction Corridors** — where factions meet, clash, trade  
- **Neutral Zones** — Outer Rim trade, guild markets  
- **Anomaly Fields** — cosmic distortions, lanternway fractures  
- **Physics Zones** — Sparkle fields, Chaos fields  
- **Sleeve‑Specific Regions** — morph‑optimized territories  
- **Narrative Domains** — Blade Runner districts, memory‑driven zones  

These categories form the **territorial backbone** of the map.

---

## **2. Territory Boundaries**  
Territories in D7–D9 do **not** use geographic borders.  
They use **dimensional gradients**.

### **Boundary Types**
- **Hard Boundaries** — faction control lines  
- **Soft Boundaries** — influence gradients  
- **Permeable Boundaries** — trade zones  
- **Chaotic Boundaries** — anomaly‑driven distortions  
- **Sparkle Boundaries** — emotional resonance fields  

### **Boundary Rules**
- Boundaries shift based on faction pressure  
- Chaos can distort boundaries  
- Sparkle can stabilize boundaries  
- NDH stability layers prevent collapse
