## **1. Purpose**
The Dreamscape Engine Entry v1.0 defines the foundational purpose, scope, and operational posture of the Dreamscape Engine. It establishes the boundaries, constraints, and safety requirements for ND‑friendly dream‑state traversal, symbolic exploration, and narrative immersion within NDH architecture.

This Entry precedes the Companion, Specification, and Manifest, and provides the initial structural anchor for all Dreamscape Engine development.

---

## **2. System Overview**
The Dreamscape Engine is a governed runtime designed to:

- enable safe dream‑state exploration  
- support symbolic and narrative environments  
- maintain ND cognitive stability  
- enforce identity boundaries  
- prevent emotional gradient instability  
- ensure symbolic containment  
- provide fork‑safe traversal  

The Engine operates strictly within NDH dimensional, cognitive, and mythic boundaries.
