# Moral Invariants Ledger v1.0

## 1. Purpose
The Moral Invariants Ledger v1.0 formally records the **ethical invariants** that govern NDH Geometry Suite v1.2 across all dimensions (3D–12D).  
It serves as the **audit trail** behind the Moral Philosophy Couch Entry v1.0, ensuring that moral commitments are explicit, enforceable, and non‑drifting.

## 3. Core Moral Invariants

### 3.1 Invariant A — Dignity
- **Statement:** Every NDH artifact must uphold the inherent dignity of individuals, especially those who are unseen.  
- **Scope:** 3D–12D, all mandates.  
- **Enforcement:** Governance Spine, HRD priority.

### 3.2 Invariant B — Safety
- **Statement:** NDH systems must never create harm, instability, or coercive pressure.  
- **Scope:** All structural and dimensional artifacts.  
- **Enforcement:** Stability Suite, 12D Safety Envelope.

### 3.3 Invariant C — Agency
- **Statement:** NDH must expand human agency, never replace or diminish it.  
- **Scope:** Interaction, automation, projection.  
- **Enforcement:** Non‑autonomy constraints, Codex lattice rules.

### 3.4 Invariant D — Clarity
- **Statement:** NDH must remain interpretable at the 3D human layer; opacity is a moral failure.  
- **Scope:** Documentation, governance, maps, diagrams.  
- **Enforcement:** Documentation Lineage (10D), Governance Clarity (11D).

### 3.5 Invariant E — Compassion
- **Statement:** Design choices must consider the lived realities of unseen people.  
- **Scope:** Architecture, policy, workflow.  
- **Enforcement:** Moral Philosophy Couch, HRD lens.

---

## 4. Moral Boundary Conditions

### 4.1 No Structural Harm
- **Rule:** No NDH structure may be used to justify or enable harm.  
- **Check:** Governance audits, risk ledgers.

### 4.2 No Dimensional Coercion
- **Rule:** Dimensional systems must not override human decision‑making.  
- **Check:** Non‑autonomy constraints, projection rules.

### 4.3 No Governance Without Human Anchor
- **Rule:** Governance abstractions must collapse back into human‑scale meaning.  
- **Check:** 3D explanatory artifacts, Philosophy Couch entries.

### 4.4 No Ethical Drift
- **Rule:** Moral invariants must remain stable across versions and dimensions.  
- **Check:** Version lineage (10D), moral diff checks.
