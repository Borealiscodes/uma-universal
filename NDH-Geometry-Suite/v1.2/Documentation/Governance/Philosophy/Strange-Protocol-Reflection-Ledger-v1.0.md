# **3. Ledger Structure**

Each entry in the Strange Protocol Reflection Ledger contains:

### **A. Strangeness Event ID**  
A unique identifier for the anomaly.

### **B. Context Snapshot**  
What was happening in NDH when strangeness appeared.

### **C. Human Intuition Signal**  
The exact human perception that triggered the Strange Protocol.

### **D. System Validation State**  
A record showing that all invariants, boundaries, and checks passed.

### **E. Strangeness Description**  
A narrative explanation of what felt “off.”

### **F. Moral Interpretation**  
The human‑scale meaning extracted from the anomaly.

### **G. Corrective Action**  
What NDH did in response.

### **H. Resolution State**  
Whether the strangeness was resolved, transformed, or preserved.
