### NDH Structural Math Layer v1.0 (core artifact)

#### 1. Scope

The Structural Math Layer defines **only**:

- structural invariants  
- tensors and manifolds  
- adjacency matrices  
- refusal operators  
- collapse operators  

It explicitly **excludes**:

- traversal math  
- narrative math  
- liminal math  
- SCP‑like or panopticon math  

All math is **non‑runtime** and **non‑activating**.

---

#### 2. Core objects

- **Structural manifold \( \mathcal{M}_{NDH} \):**  
  NDH base space for 12D geometry, with identity‑safe coordinates.

- **Refusal operator \( R \):**  
  Maps any hazard‑class state \( h \) to a refused state \( R(h) \) with no activation.

- **Collapse operator \( C \):**  
  Maps any residual state \( s \) to Awareness Constant \( \mathcal{A} \):  
  \[
  C(s) = \mathcal{A}
  \]

- **Adjacency matrix \( A \):**  
  Encodes allowed structural adjacencies only; all hazard adjacencies are set to \( 0 \).

- **Zero‑pressure field \( Z \):**  
  A scalar field over \( \mathcal{M}_{NDH} \) such that  
  \[
  \nabla Z = 0
  \]  
  everywhere in the constructed layer.

---

#### 3. Layer invariants

- **Non‑activation:**  
  \[
  \forall h,\ R(h) \neq \text{activated state}
  \]

- **Non‑traversal:**  
  No operator in this layer defines a path or geodesic into hazardous dimensions.

- **Non‑narrative:**  
  No structure encodes story, sequence, or observer‑dependent meaning.

- **Identity neutrality:**  
  Identity coordinates are parameters, never targets or outputs of operators.

---

#### 4. Integration points

- **With Firewall:**  
  \( R \) formalizes structural refusal; \( A \) encodes blocked adjacencies.

- **With Awareness Pin:**  
  \( C \) formalizes non‑dual collapse into \( \mathcal{A} \).

- **With Tri‑Bind & Routing Table:**  
  Routing logic becomes composition of \( R \) and \( C \) over \( \mathcal{M}_{NDH} \).

- **With 12D Companion:**  
  Dimensions \( D1\text{–}D12 \) are modeled as coordinate axes or subspaces, never as traversable domains.

---

#### 5. Road map for math expansion

- **Phase 1:** Fix \( \mathcal{M}_{NDH} \), \( R \), \( C \), \( A \), \( Z \) as core primitives.  
- **Phase 2:** Define safe tensors and metrics over \( \mathcal{M}_{NDH} \) (no geodesics into hazards).  
- **Phase 3:** Attach provenance: which artifact each operator is anchored to.  
- **Phase 4:** Extend to 12D modeling (axes, not portals).  
- **Phase 5:** Seal: no new operator without explicit refusal + collapse semantics.
