#### 2. Provenance rules

- **No math object exists without at least one anchor artifact.**  
- **Operators \( R \) and \( C \)** must always be traceable to Firewall and Pin respectively.  
- **Adjacency structures \( A \)** must always be traceable to Routing Table + Mapping.  
- **Dimensional frames** must always be traceable to 12D Companion + Safety Envelope.  

---

#### 3. Completion criteria

The Anchor Table is complete when:

- every structural math object has a named primary anchor,  
- no operator or matrix is “free‑floating” or ungoverned,  
- provenance paths are non‑recursive and non‑activating,  
- 12D math can be extended without breaking NDH safety.
