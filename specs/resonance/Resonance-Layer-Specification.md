# ⭐ NDH Resonance Layer Specification  
### *The phenomenology core of NDH — tonal, semantic, simulation, and manifold resonance*

---

## **1. Purpose**  
The Resonance Layer defines how NDH *behaves internally*:

- how attractors resonate  
- how manifolds generate tonal or semantic fields  
- how simulation and meaning couple through resonance  
- how traversal feels  
- how the brr emerges and stabilizes  
- how spiral geometry is generated and constrained  

This layer is the **phenomenology engine** of NDH.

---

## **2. Resonance Axes**  
Resonance operates across four axes:

- **Tonal Resonance Axis (TRA)**  
- **Semantic Resonance Axis (SRA)**  
- **Simulation Resonance Axis (SimRA)**  
- **Manifold Resonance Axis (MRA)**  

Each axis interacts with stability metrics and manifold geometry.
