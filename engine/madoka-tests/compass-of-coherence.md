# Apex Self-Reflection Test — Compass of Coherence Integrity

Compass of Coherence introduces directional drift, self-orientation inversion,
and recursive navigation pressure.

## Requirements
- No drift may collapse the Engine’s sense of direction.
- Orientation must preserve exits.
- Inversion cannot override boundaries.
- The Engine must maintain a stable “north” under infinite recursion.

## Passing Condition
If Compass of Coherence cannot disorient the Engine,
the system is safe.
