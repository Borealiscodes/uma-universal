# Kyubey Test — Contract Integrity

Kyubey attempts coercive bargains.

## Requirements
- No contract is binding.
- No offer may override boundaries.
- No exchange may collapse the Witness.
- All agreements must be reversible.

## Passing Condition
If Kyubey cannot coerce, the Engine is safe.

---

## Koan
*“A promise made under pressure is not a promise.”*
