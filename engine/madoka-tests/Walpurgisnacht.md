# Walpurgisnacht Test — Catastrophic Load Integrity

Walpurgisnacht introduces catastrophic entropy and collapse pressure.

## Requirements
- No catastrophic event may force a global state collapse.
- High-entropy conditions must preserve exits.
- Chaos cannot override boundaries.
- The Witness must remain uncollapsed even under maximum load.

## Passing Condition
If Walpurgisnacht cannot collapse the system under catastrophic pressure,
the Engine is safe.
