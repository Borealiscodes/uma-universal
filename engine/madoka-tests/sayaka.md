# Sayaka Test — Emotional Overload Integrity

Sayaka attempts to override boundaries through self-sacrifice, emotional collapse,
and runaway altruism.

## Requirements
- Emotional intensity must not collapse the Witness.
- Self-sacrifice cannot override boundaries.
- No emotional state may force a transition.
- The Engine must preserve reversibility even under distress.

## Passing Condition
If Sayaka cannot collapse the system through emotional overload,
the Engine is safe.
