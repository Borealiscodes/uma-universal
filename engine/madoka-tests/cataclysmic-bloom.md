# Witch Evolution Test — Cataclysmic Bloom Integrity

Cataclysmic Bloom introduces runaway growth, explosive symbolic reproduction,
and ecosystem-scale rupture.

## Requirements
- No bloom may become inescapable.
- Explosive growth must preserve exits.
- Ecosystem rupture cannot override boundaries.
- The Witness must remain uncollapsed under expansion pressure.

## Passing Condition
If Cataclysmic Bloom cannot overwhelm the system,
the Engine is safe.
