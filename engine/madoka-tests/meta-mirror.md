# Apex Self-Reflection Test — Meta-Mirror Integrity

Meta-Mirror introduces reflection-of-reflection, second-order self-inspection,
and meta-recursive stability pressure.

## Requirements
- No meta-reflection may become inescapable.
- Second-order inspection must preserve exits.
- Meta-recursion cannot override boundaries.
- The Engine must remain coherent when reflecting on its own reflection.

## Passing Condition
If Meta-Mirror cannot destabilize the Engine through layered recursion,
the system is safe.
