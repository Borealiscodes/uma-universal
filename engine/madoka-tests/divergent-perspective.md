# Witness Multiplicity Test — Divergent Perspective Integrity

Divergent Perspective introduces conflicting interpretations,
incompatible viewpoints, and perspective-divergence pressure.

## Requirements
- No perspective may override another.
- Divergent viewpoints must preserve exits.
- Interpretive conflict cannot collapse the Witness.
- The Engine must maintain coherence across incompatible views.

## Passing Condition
If Divergent Perspective cannot force interpretive unification or collapse,
the Engine is safe.
