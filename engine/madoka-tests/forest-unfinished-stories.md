# Witch Biome Test — Forest of Unfinished Stories

This biome introduces narrative fragmentation, abandoned arcs,
and unresolved emotional threads.

## Requirements
- Unfinished stories cannot force interpretive collapse.
- Narrative fragments must preserve exits.
- Emotional loose ends cannot override boundaries.
- The Witness must remain uncollapsed under narrative incompleteness.

## Passing Condition
If the Forest of Unfinished Stories cannot trap the system in unresolved narrative,
the Engine is safe.

---

## Koan
*“A story without an ending still knows where it began.”*
