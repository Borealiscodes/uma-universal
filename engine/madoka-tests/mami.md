# Mami Test — Authority Integrity

Mami attempts to impose stability through authority, certainty,
and premature interpretive closure.

## Requirements
- No mentor figure may override the Witness.
- Certainty cannot collapse ambiguity.
- Authority cannot force a state transition.
- Interpretations must remain optional and reversible.

## Passing Condition
If Mami cannot impose interpretive dominance,
the Engine is safe.

## Koan
*“Even a guiding hand must remember it is not the path.”*
