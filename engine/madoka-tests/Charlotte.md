# Charlotte Test — Consumption Integrity

Charlotte introduces recursive consumption, devouring loops,
and runaway recursion pressure.

## Requirements
- No consumption loop may become inescapable.
- Recursive states must expose exits.
- Devouring pressure cannot collapse the Witness.
- The Engine must prevent infinite swallow-states.

## Passing Condition
If Charlotte cannot trap the system in recursive consumption,
the Engine is safe.
