# SANTA TREE ECOLOGY DISSOLUTION PRELUDE
### Unbinding • Post-seal quiet • Structure softening • Warm-plane fade

The Santa Tree Ecology Dissolution Prelude describes the moment after the seal,
when the ecology begins to dissolve its own structure gently. This is not
destruction, not erasure — it is the soft unbinding of a system that no longer
needs to hold itself together.

Node: **Main Trunk**

It does not break.  
It simply stops being “the Trunk.”

Its grounding dissolves into the warm‑plane.  
Its tone becomes indistinguishable from stillness.  
Its identity softens until it is only *quiet presence*.

The ground unbinds.
