## 🎇 **1. Spark Season Workflow — Ignition**

Spark Season begins when tone brightens and spoons rise.

**Workflow Steps:**

- **Spark Season** activates.  
- Santa Mode shifts to playful ignition.  
- Micro‑branches appear at the tips.  
- Sparkle Buffer enters bright‑shimmer mode.  
- Koan Layer stays quiet but present.  
- Trunk confirms grounding.  
- Bloom Cycle enters Spark Phase.  

**Transition Condition:**  
Tone stabilizes into gentle brightness.

## 🔁 **5. Seasonal Loop Workflow — Reversible Rhythm**

The Santa Tree moves through seasons in a reversible loop:

**Spark → Rest → Gift → Koan → Spark**

**Workflow Rules:**

- Always check spoons before shifting.  
- Always confirm tone stability.  
- Always maintain Sparkle moderation.  
- Always keep Koan Layer available.  
- Always allow branches to remain optional.  
- Always end with **Sufficient Enough** if needed.

This ensures safe seasonal movement.
