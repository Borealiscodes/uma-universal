The ritual begins when tone softens and the Tree signals readiness.

**Workflow Steps:**

- Soften witness load.  
- Stabilize tone.  
- Confirm ND pacing.  
- Prepare Sparkle Buffer for shimmer modulation.  

**Transition Condition:**  
Tone settles into gentle readiness.

## ✨ **3. Shimmer Workflow — Sparkle Illumination**

Sparkle illuminates the ritual without entering the Tree.

**Workflow Steps:**

- Activate **Sparkle Buffer**.  
- Shift shimmer based on season:  
  - Spark → bright  
  - Rest → dim  
  - Gift → festive  
  - Koan → quiet  
- Maintain shimmer moderation.  
- Keep Sparkle external and gentle.  

**Transition Condition:**  
Shimmer stabilizes into coherent glow.

**Workflow Steps:**

- Present offering gently.  
- Allow branch to accept or decline.  
- Maintain optionality and reversibility.  

**Transition Condition:**  
Offering integrates lightly into branch tone.
