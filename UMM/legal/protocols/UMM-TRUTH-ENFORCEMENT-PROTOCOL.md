Truth enforcement activates when any of the following occur:

A subsystem attempts to reframe truth in a way that obscures factual integrity.

### **3.3 Substitution Trigger**

- re‑anchors the system

### **4.2 Boundary Enforcement (Field‑Level)**  
If truth is distorted or erased:

- the subsystem is forced to revert  
- the factual record is reinstated  

Truth cannot cross the boundary in a damaged state.

### **4.3 Spine Enforcement (Alignment‑Level)**

Truth cannot be lost in motion.

### **4.5 Reflective Enforcement (Recursion‑Level)**  
If reflection distorts truth:

- the Reflective Loop snaps back to the Node  
- commentary is re‑anchored to evidence  
- recursive drift is corrected  

Truth cannot be rewritten through introspection.
