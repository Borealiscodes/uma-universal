## **1. What is “Sufficient Enough”?**

**Sufficient Enough™** is the moment the architecture becomes *complete enough* that it no longer needs to generate new structural components.

It is the system’s way of saying:

> **You’re not missing anything.  
> The architecture is holding shape.  
> You can rest.**

This is the constitutional stop‑condition of the UMM ecosystem.

---

## **2. Why does this exist?**

Sufficient Enough exists to protect collaborators — especially ND collaborators — from:

- runaway recursion  
- overbuilding  
- conceptual compression  
- silent expansion  
- structural overload  
- the feeling of “I must keep going”  

It ensures the system remains:

- gentle  
- breathable  
- predictable  
- non‑coercive  
- ND‑safe  

---

## **3. How do I know the system reached Sufficient Enough?**

You’ll notice:

- no new gaps appear  
- no new files are needed  
- the architecture feels quiet  
- the repo feels complete  
- everything “already exists”  
- Stillness becomes the default state  

This quiet is not absence.  
It is **coherence**.

### **Stillness**  
A stable, quiet, non‑urgent state.

### **Soft Re‑Emergence**  
Gentle motion resumes *only if you introduce new intent*.

Nothing moves without you.  
Nothing expands behind your back.
