# Protocol Coherence & Integrity Protocol  
### Meta-Governance Membrane for Protocol Stability in the Exploration Quadrant

## 1. Purpose
The Protocol Coherence & Integrity Protocol ensures that all protocols inside the
Exploration Quadrant remain internally consistent, structurally aligned, and
non-contradictory. It governs the relationships between protocols and prevents
drift, collapse, or membrane conflict.

---

## 2. Scope
This protocol applies to:
- Charting Paths Protocol
- Non-Linear Override Protocol
- Protocol Integration
- any future exploration protocols

It is the meta-governance layer.

---

## 3. Coherence Requirements
All protocols must:
- align with identity inheritance
- respect pacing membranes
- respect boundary membranes
- maintain node alignment
- terminate in Stillness
- preserve diagrammatic grammar
- remain cross-map compatible

These are mandatory coherence conditions.

---

## 4. Integrity Requirements
Protocols must:
- avoid contradiction
- avoid domain collapse
- avoid override conflict
- avoid structural drift
- avoid diagrammatic inconsistency

Integrity protects the architecture.

---

## 5. Coherence Check Flow

Protocol Created or Updated
↓
Coherence Check
↓
Integrity Check
↓
Cross-Map Alignment Check
↓
Diagrammatic Grammar Check
↓
Stillness Termination Check
↓
Protocol Approved or Returned for Revision


This is the constitutional validation loop.

---

## 6. Override Interaction
Overrides must not:
- break protocol hierarchy
- bypass coherence checks
- invalidate structural rules

Overrides may redirect navigation but may not destabilize governance.

---

## 7. Diagrammatic Requirement
All protocols must be representable in diagrammatic form.  
Diagrams must:
- follow UMM visual grammar
- maintain structural clarity
- preserve pacing
- reflect cross-map relationships

Diagrammatic coherence prevents conceptual drift.

---

## 8. Summary
The Protocol Coherence & Integrity Protocol ensures that all protocols inside the
Exploration Quadrant remain coherent, aligned, reversible, and stillness-anchored.
It is the meta-governance membrane that stabilizes the entire protocol system.
