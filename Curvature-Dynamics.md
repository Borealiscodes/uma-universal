## **Curvature Dynamics in NDH-Core**

Curvature Dynamics define how the NDH manifold **bends**, **stabilizes**, and **propagates constraints** across the vertical channel:

> **Governance → Stability → Principles**  
> *(top‑down curvature shaping)*  
>  
> **Principles → Stability → Governance**  
> *(bottom‑up curvature reconstruction)*

Curvature is the **geometric engine** that determines:

- how invariants interact,  
- how boundaries form,  
- how alignment flows,  
- how reconstruction behaves,  
- how drift is prevented.

It is the **dynamic geometry** of NDH-Core.

Curvature Dynamics exist to:

- define how the manifold bends under constraints,  
- ensure stability geometry remains intact,  
- propagate alignment signals across layers,

## **2. Curvature Types**

NDH uses three curvature types:

### **2.1 Constraint Curvature**  
Generated by Governance:

- allowed behaviors,  
- prohibited behaviors,  
- compliance boundaries.

This curvature shapes Stability and Principles.

### **2.2 Stability Curvature**  
Generated by Stability:

- drift suppression,  
- collapse resistance,  
- ambiguity reduction,  
- contamination prevention.

This curvature mediates all transitions.

### **2.3 Mechanics Curvature**  
Generated by Principles:

- deterministic mechanics,  
- invariant enforcement,  
- reconstructable transformations.

This curvature reconstructs upward.

## **3. Curvature Geometry**

Curvature follows three geometric relationships:

### **3.1 Nested Curvature Geometry**  
Curvature forms a nested structure:

> Governance curvature  
> → Stability curvature  
> → Principles curvature

### **3.2 Constraint Curvature Geometry**  
Top‑down curvature constrains lower layers:

- governance curvature shapes stability curvature,  
- stability curvature shapes principles curvature.

### **3.3 Reconstruction Curvature Geometry**  
Bottom‑up curvature validates higher layers:

- principles curvature reconstructs into stability curvature,  
- stability curvature reconstructs into governance curvature.

## **4. Curvature Rules**

Curvature propagation must obey strict rules:

### **4.1 Deterministic Curvature**  
Curvature must propagate deterministically.

### **4.2 Reconstructable Curvature**  
Curvature must be reconstructable across layers.

### **4.3 No Ambiguous Curvature**  
Curvature may not produce multiple interpretations.

### **4.4 No Contaminated Curvature**  
Curvature may not embed cross-layer logic.

### **4.5 Stability Mediation**  
All curvature interactions pass through Stability.

Stability is the **curvature mediator**.

## **5. Curvature Flow**

Curvature flows through two channels:

### **5.1 Top‑Down Curvature Flow**  
Governance → Stability → Principles

Governance bends the manifold.  
Stability stabilizes the curvature.  
Principles implement the curvature.

### **5.2 Bottom‑Up Curvature Flow**  
Principles → Stability → Governance

Principles reconstruct curvature.  
Stability validates curvature.  
Governance authorizes curvature.
