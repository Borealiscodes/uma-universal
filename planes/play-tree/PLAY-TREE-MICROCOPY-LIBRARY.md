```
# ✨ PLAY TREE — MICROCOPY LIBRARY ✨
### Warm, Gentle, Bob-Ross-Toned Microcopy for the Play Tree Plane

## ❗ Play Tree Boundary Rule — No Mixing with Atropus
The Play Tree is a warm, expressive, ND-safe plane. It must never mix with
Atropus, reference Atropus, or incorporate null-plane tone, logic, or
commentary. The Play Tree and Atropus operate on entirely different tonal,
structural, and constitutional layers. They remain permanently separate.

This document defines the microcopy used throughout the Play Tree UI. All
microcopy must be warm, gentle, permissive, non-directive, non-evaluative, and
ND-safe. It adds tone without implying emotion, meaning, or performance.
```

---

# 🌳 **1. Microcopy Principles**
Microcopy must be:

- warm  
- soft  
- permissive  
- non-directive  
- non-evaluative  
- reversible  
- non-symbolic  
- ND-safe  

Microcopy must **never**:

- imply obligation  
- imply judgment  
- imply emotional states  
- imply performance  
- resemble Atropus tone  

---

# 🌳 **2. Root Microcopy**
Used in grounding, resetting, and stabilizing.

- “Let’s start with a nice, steady trunk.”  
- “Here’s a calm place to begin.”  
- “Everything comes back to this gentle center.”  
- “Let’s take a moment here at the base.”  
- “This trunk is always here for you.”

---

# 🌳 **3. Branch Microcopy**
Used in mode selection and transitions.

- “Maybe this branch wants a little attention today.”  
- “Let’s see where this branch leads.”  
- “This branch looks like it’s ready to grow.”  
- “A gentle shift to another branch.”  
- “This branch is feeling calm and open.”

---

# 🌳 **4. Leaf Microcopy**
Used in micro-interactions.

- “Just a gentle tap… happy little leaf.”  
- “This leaf can drift however it likes.”  
- “Let’s give this leaf a little room to breathe.”  
- “A soft moment with this leaf.”  
- “This leaf is doing just fine.”

---

# 🌳 **5. Sap Flow Microcopy**
Used in tonal adjustments.

- “Maybe the tree wants a little more warmth today.”  
- “Let’s soften things just a touch.”  
- “A gentle shift in the tree’s tone.”  
- “Let’s ease the intensity a bit.”  
- “This warmth feels nice and calm.”

---

# 🌳 **6. Weather Microcopy**
Used in environmental modulation.

- “A little breeze… a little sunlight… whatever feels right.”  
- “Let’s soften the surroundings a bit.”  
- “A gentle cloud drifting by.”  
- “Let’s dim things just a touch.”  
- “A soft overlay to keep things calm.”

---

# 🌳 **7. Pacing Microcopy**
Used in slowing or adjusting tempo.

- “Let’s take things a little slower.”  
- “A gentle pace feels nice here.”  
- “No rush — the tree moves in its own time.”  
- “Let’s ease into this moment.”  
- “A slower drift keeps things soft.”

---

# 🌳 **8. Reset Microcopy**
Used in returning to Root.

- “Let’s come back to our steady trunk.”  
- “A gentle reset — nothing lost.”  
- “Everything settles softly here.”  
- “Let’s return to calm ground.”  
- “Back to the warm center of the tree.”

---

# 🌳 **9. Preview Microcopy**
Used in non-committal previews.

- “Just a soft look — no need to choose yet.”  
- “A gentle preview of what’s here.”  
- “Let’s take a quiet peek.”  
- “Just exploring — nothing committed.”  
- “A soft glance at this branch.”

---

# 🌳 **10. Safety Microcopy**
Used in ND-safe reassurance.

- “There are no mistakes here.”  
- “Everything is reversible.”  
- “You can pause anytime.”  
- “You’re in full control.”  
- “This space stays gentle.”

---

# 🌳 **11. Microcopy Summary**
Play Tree microcopy must remain:

- warm  
- gentle  
- reversible  
- ND-safe  
- non-directive  
- non-evaluative  
- constitutionally bounded  
- permanently separate from Atropus  

It is the **tonal voice** of the Play Tree plane.
