# 🌳 **1. Purpose of the Play Tree Plane**
The Play Tree exists to provide:

- a **warm**, **gentle**, **ND-safe** interaction environment  
- a **multi-layered structure** for exploration and expression  
- a **reversible**, **non-coercive** interaction model  
- a **soft**, **calming**, **Bob‑Ross‑toned** experience  
- a **clear constitutional boundary** between layers  

The Play Tree is **not**:

- emotional  
- symbolic  
- narrative  
- psychological  

It is a **functional plane** for interaction and pacing.

# 🌳 **4. Plane Invariants**
The Play Tree has **six constitutional invariants**:

### **4.1 Reversibility**
Every action must be undoable.

### **4.2 Non-Coercion**
The plane must never force behavior.

### **4.3 Softness**
All transitions must be gentle.

### **4.4 Warmth**
The plane must maintain a warm tonal profile.

### **4.5 Layer Separation**
Layers must not collapse into each other.

### **4.6 No Atropus Mixing**
The Play Tree must remain permanently separate from Atropus.
