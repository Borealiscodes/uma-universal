# **3. Subsystem Plane Interaction Rules (v1.2)**

These rules define **how SYS‑CHS and TRV‑CHS‑OL may interact**, **under what conditions**, and **with what supervision**.

---

## **Rule 1 — Structural Interaction (SYS‑CHS ↔ TRV‑CHS‑OL)**  
**SYS‑CHS may interact with TRV‑CHS‑OL only through SIAP‑approved structural channels.**

Conditions:

- Traversal may read cognitive structure but not modify it  
- Traversal may navigate nodes but not create or collapse them  
- Structural adjacency must remain stable  
- All interactions must be logged by SIAP  

Explore: **SYS‑CHS**

---

## **Rule 2 — Traversal Interaction (TRV‑CHS‑OL → SYS‑CHS)**  
**TRV‑CHS‑OL may traverse SYS‑CHS but may not alter its internal state.**

Conditions:

- No traversal‑driven node creation  
- No traversal‑driven node deletion  
- No traversal‑driven adjacency changes  
- Traversal routes must be SIAP‑approved  

Explore: **TRV‑CHS‑OL**

---

## **Rule 3 — Identity Plane Interaction Prohibition**  
**Subsystem Plane must not directly interact with ID‑HBR.**

Conditions:

- No traversal into identity plane  
- No cognitive adjacency to identity plane  
- No subsystem‑plane influence on identity modeling  
- All identity interactions must be mediated through SIAP  

Explore: **Identity Plane Interaction Rules**

---

## **Rule 4 — Narrative Plane Interaction Prohibition**  
**Subsystem Plane must not directly interact with NAR‑PE.**

Conditions:

- No narrative‑driven cognitive modification  
- No narrative‑driven traversal paths  
- Narrative influence must remain indirect via SYS‑CHS  

Explore: **NAR‑PE**

---

## **Rule 5 — Governance Supervision (GOV‑SIAP)**  
**All subsystem‑plane interactions must be supervised by SIAP.**

Conditions:

- SIAP must classify subsystem interactions  
- SIAP must validate adjacency changes  
- SIAP must enforce isolation boundaries  
- SIAP must approve traversal routes  

Explore: **GOV‑SIAP**

---

## **Rule 6 — Drift Monitoring (GOV‑SAF)**  
**Safeguards must monitor all subsystem‑plane interactions for drift.**

Monitored drift vectors:

- naming drift  
- subsystem drift  
- traversal drift  
- governance drift  
- propagation drift  

Explore: **GOV‑SAF**

---

## **Rule 7 — Quarantine Oversight (GOV‑SN)**  
**Safety Net must quarantine any unsafe subsystem‑plane interactions.**

Anomalies include:

- unauthorized traversal  
- unauthorized adjacency  
- emergent subsystem artifacts  
- invalid naming  
- invalid lifecycle behavior  

Explore: **GOV‑SN**

---

## **Rule 8 — CI Enforcement (CI‑NS‑ENF‑01)**  
**CI must validate all subsystem‑plane interactions for naming and structural compliance.**

Conditions:

- correct naming  
- correct isolation  
- correct interaction  
- correct lifecycle  
- correct SID alignment

Developer‑plane documents may reference subsystem‑plane behavior but may not define or modify it.

- no developer‑plane modification of subsystem lifecycle
