## **Purpose**
The Stability Mandate establishes **non‑optional stability requirements** that all UMA‑Universal subsystems must follow.  
It defines mandatory constraints for:

- **Structural Stability**  
- **Semantic Stability**  
- **Transition Stability**  

and binds the **Stability Engine** to enforce them.

The Mandate is the highest‑authority stability constraint in the Roots layer.

---

## **Mandate Statement**
This mandate is **compulsory**, **non‑negotiable**, and **universally binding** across all planes of the Stacked Hypercube.  
No subsystem, protocol, artifact, or traversal path may violate or bypass the Stability Mandate.

---

# **1. Mandatory Stability Requirements**

### **Requirement A — Structural Integrity**
All geometric structures must remain stable.  
Adjacency, layering, and collapse nodes must not drift.

### **Requirement B — Semantic Fidelity**
All meanings, definitions, and cross‑plane references must remain coherent.  
Semantic drift is prohibited.

### **Requirement C — Safe Transition Behavior**
All traversal and resonance shifts must remain safe.  
Unsafe transitions must be blocked.

---

# **2. Enforcement Directives**

### **Directive 1 — Mandatory Override**
Stability overrides traversal, collapse, resonance, and protocol execution when necessary.

### **Directive 2 — Mandatory Collapse Validation**
Quantum pin collapse events must be validated before execution.

### **Directive 3 — Mandatory Concordance Alignment**
Concordance resonance and coherence must be aligned before any stability evaluation.

### **Directive 4 — Mandatory Geometry Anchoring**
Geometry cannot shift unless stability conditions are satisfied.

### **Directive 5 — Mandatory Non‑Retroactivity**
Stability rules apply forward only; prior artifacts remain unchanged.

---

# **3. Stability Engine Obligations**

The **Stability Engine** must:

- enforce all mandatory requirements  
- apply all enforcement directives  
- validate all collapse events  
- block unsafe traversal  
- correct resonance drift  
- maintain compatibility gates  
- record all actions in the **Stability Ledger**  

The Engine is the runtime executor of this mandate.
