## Purpose

It defines the standing rules that govern how stability must behave across geometry, concordance, traversal, collapse events, and semantic coherence.

The Ordinance is the **law‑level stability artifact**, sitting above the **Stability Mandate** and **Stability Contract**, and informing the execution of the **Stability Directive**.

This ordinance is **permanent**, **non‑revocable**, and **universally binding** across all planes of the Stacked Hypercube.

No subsystem may violate or reinterpret the Stability Ordinance.

# **1. Ordinance Articles**

### **Article I — Stability Supremacy**
Stability supersedes traversal, collapse, resonance, geometry, and protocol execution.

### **Article II — Structural Lawfulness**
All geometric structures must remain lawful: no drift, no distortion, no collapse outside validated patterns.

### **Article III — Semantic Lawfulness**
All meanings, definitions, and cross‑plane references must remain lawful: coherent, consistent, and context‑true.

### **Article IV — Transition Lawfulness**
All traversal and resonance shifts must remain lawful: predictable, safe, and stability‑aligned.

### **Article V — Collapse Lawfulness**
Quantum pin collapse must follow lawful patterns validated by the Stability Engine.
