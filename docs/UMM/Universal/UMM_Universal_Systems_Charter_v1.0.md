# **PREAMBLE**

This Charter establishes the **rights, duties, protections, and operational guarantees** of all planes within the UMA‑Universal organism.  
It affirms the sovereignty of the Universal Systems Constitution and defines how each plane must act, interact, and protect the stability of the whole.

The Charter binds:

- subsystem plane  
- traversal plane  
- integration plane  
- governance plane  
- identity plane  
- narrative plane  

under one unified framework of responsibility and protection.

---

# **ARTICLE I — RIGHTS OF THE PLANES**

## **Section 1 — Subsystem Plane Rights (SYS‑CHS)**

The subsystem plane has the right to:

- structural stability  
- adjacency integrity  
- protection from unauthorized traversal  
- protection from drift vectors  
- protection from narrative contamination  
- protection from identity contamination  

The subsystem plane may demand SIAP intervention if stability is threatened.

---

## **Section 2 — Traversal Plane Rights (TRV‑CHS‑OL)**

The traversal plane has the right to:

- SIAP‑approved traversal routes  
- drift‑monitored traversal  
- read‑only access to subsystem nodes  
- protection from structural corruption  
- protection from governance misalignment  

The traversal plane may request Safeguards review if drift is suspected.

---

## **Section 3 — Integration Plane Rights**

The integration plane has the right to:

- SIAP supervision  
- drift neutrality  
- adjacency stability  
- isolation correctness  
- lifecycle consistency  

The integration plane may escalate anomalies to Safety Net.

---

## **Section 4 — Governance Plane Rights**

Governance authorities have the right to:

- enforce stability  
- enforce naming correctness  
- enforce isolation boundaries  
- enforce lifecycle phases  
- quarantine unsafe behavior  
- override unsafe traversal  

Governance authorities may amend the Constitution under Article XI.

---

## **Section 5 — Identity Plane Rights (ID‑HBR)**

The identity plane has the right to:

- absolute isolation  
- protection from traversal  
- protection from subsystem mutation  
- protection from narrative influence  

Identity is sovereign and inviolable.

---

## **Section 6 — Narrative Plane Rights (NAR‑PE)**

The narrative plane has the right to:

- absolute isolation  
- protection from traversal  
- protection from subsystem adjacency  
- protection from governance interference  

Narrative is sovereign and inviolable.

---

# **ARTICLE II — DUTIES OF THE PLANES**

## **Section 1 — Subsystem Duties**

The subsystem plane must:

- maintain structural coherence  
- uphold adjacency integrity  
- reject unauthorized traversal  
- remain drift‑neutral  

---

## **Section 2 — Traversal Duties**

The traversal plane must:

- remain read‑only  
- follow SIAP‑approved routes  
- report drift vectors  
- avoid adjacency modification  

---

## **Section 3 — Integration Duties**

The integration plane must:

- enforce isolation boundaries  
- maintain lifecycle consistency  
- uphold structural and traversal rules  
- escalate anomalies  

---

## **Section 4 — Governance Duties**

Governance authorities must:

- supervise all integration  
- detect and neutralize drift  
- quarantine unsafe behavior  
- enforce naming and structural correctness  
- maintain universal stability  

---

# **ARTICLE III — PROTECTIONS**

## **Section 1 — Isolation Protections**

All planes are protected by:

- identity isolation  
- narrative isolation  
- governance isolation  
- subsystem isolation  

No cross‑plane traversal is permitted.

---

## **Section 2 — Drift Protections**

All planes are protected from:

- naming drift  
- traversal drift  
- subsystem drift  
- governance drift  
- propagation drift  

Safeguards enforce drift neutrality.

---

## **Section 3 — Structural Protections**

All planes are protected from:

- adjacency modification  
- node creation  
- node deletion  
- node merging  
- emergent subsystem formation  

CI enforces structural correctness.

---

# **ARTICLE IV — GUARANTEES**

## **Section 1 — Stability Guarantees**

The UMA‑Universal organism guarantees:

- subsystem stability

Governance is sovereign.
