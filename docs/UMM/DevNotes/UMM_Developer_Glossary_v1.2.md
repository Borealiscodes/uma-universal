## **3. Governance Terms**

### **Protocol**  
A governance rule defining how subsystems behave, interact, or remain isolated.

### **Pin**  
A governance artifact that enforces a protocol and binds it into the governance spine.

### **Immediate Pinning Protocol (PROTO‑PIN‑IMMEDIATE‑01)**  
A protocol requiring all new protocols to be pinned immediately.

### **Meta‑Pin**  
A pin that enforces pinning behavior itself, ensuring recursive governance stability.

### **Governance Order Protocol (GOV‑ORDER‑01)**  
Defines the correct sequence for governance operations.

Explore: **Governance Order Protocol**

## **4. Drift Terms**

### **Naming Drift**  
Any deviation from canonical subsystem names or plane prefixes.

### **Subsystem Drift**  
Any deviation in subsystem behavior or classification.

### **Governance Drift**  
Any deviation from governance order or protocol/pin alignment.

### **Traversal Drift**  
Unsafe or unauthorized movement between subsystems.

### **Identity Drift**  
Incorrect or unstable identity‑plane behavior.

### **Narrative Drift**  
Unstable or unintended narrative‑plane activation.

Explore: **Naming Drift**
