# **3. Identity Plane Interaction Rules (v1.2)**

These rules define **how ID‑HBR may interact**, **under what conditions**, and **with what supervision**.

---

## **Rule 1 — Cognitive Interaction (SYS‑CHS ↔ ID‑HBR)**  
**ID‑HBR may interact with SYS‑CHS for identity modeling and cognitive scaffolding.**

Conditions:

- Interaction must be structural, not traversal  
- Interaction must be mediated through SIAP classification  
- Interaction must not modify SYS‑CHS behavior  

Explore: **SYS‑CHS**

---

## **Rule 2 — Traversal Prohibition (TRV‑CHS‑OL → ID‑HBR)**  
**ID‑HBR may not interact directly with traversal subsystems.**

Conditions:

- No traversal into or out of ID‑HBR  
- No traversal‑driven identity modification  
- No traversal‑driven adjacency changes  

Explore: **TRV‑CHS‑OL**

---

## **Rule 3 — Narrative Influence (NAR‑PE → ID‑HBR)**  
**NAR‑PE may influence ID‑HBR indirectly but may not directly couple to it.**

Conditions:

- Influence must be loose, not structural  
- Influence must be mediated through SYS‑CHS  
- Influence must not modify identity‑plane boundaries  

Explore: **NAR‑PE**

---

## **Rule 4 — Governance Supervision (GOV‑SIAP → ID‑HBR)**  
**All interactions involving ID‑HBR must be supervised by SIAP.**

Conditions:

- SIAP must classify all identity‑plane interactions  
- SIAP must validate adjacency changes  
- SIAP must enforce isolation boundaries  

Explore: **GOV‑SIAP**

---

## **Rule 5 — Drift Monitoring (GOV‑SAF → ID‑HBR)**  
**Safeguards must monitor all identity‑plane interactions for drift.**

Conditions:

- Naming drift detection  
- Identity drift detection  
- Governance drift detection  
- Propagation drift detection  

Explore: **GOV‑SAF**

---

## **Rule 6 — Quarantine Oversight (GOV‑SN → ID‑HBR)**  
**Safety Net must quarantine any unsafe identity‑plane interactions.**

Conditions:

- Invalid adjacency  
- Invalid naming  
- Invalid traversal attempts  
- Emergent subsystem artifacts  

Explore: **GOV‑SN**

---

## **Rule 7 — CI Enforcement (CI‑NS‑ENF‑01)**  
**CI must validate all identity‑plane interactions for naming and structural compliance.**

Conditions:

- ID‑HBR must be referenced correctly  
- HRB must be rejected  
- Interaction rules must be respected  
- SID alignment must be maintained  

Explore: **CI Naming Enforcement**

---

## **Rule 8 — Developer‑Plane Interaction Constraints**  
**Developer‑plane documents may reference ID‑HBR but may not define or modify its interactions.**

Conditions:

- Developer‑plane artifacts are consumers, not governors  
- Interaction rules must be enforced by governance plane  
- No developer‑plane subsystem modification allowed  

Explore: **Developer Handbook v1.2**
