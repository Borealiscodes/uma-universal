# Top-Level Workflow Template  
### A Gentle, Bob-Ross Style Guide for Designing Your Own Master Workflow

## 1. Purpose
This template helps you design a calm, structured, low-pressure workflow for building your system, project, or architecture.  
It is descriptive, not prescriptive.  
It is a guide, not a rule.

---

## 2. Step 1 — Choose Your Region
Pick the part of your project you want to work on today.

Examples:
- [Core Structure](ca://s?q=Define_Core_Structure)
- [Operational Logic](ca://s?q=Define_Operational_Logic)
- [Stability Layer](ca://s?q=Define_Stability_Layer)
- [Documentation](ca://s?q=Define_Documentation)
- [Release or Publishing](ca://s?q=Define_Release_Process)

Choose gently.  
No urgency.

---

## 3. Step 2 — Identify a Small Gap
Look at the region you chose and find one tiny gap.

Examples:
- a missing definition  
- a boundary that needs clarity  
- a relationship that needs one sentence  
- a rule that needs a soft constraint  

Keep it small.  
Happy little gaps.

---

## 4. Step 3 — Pick a Friendly Brush
Choose the smallest tool that fits the gap.

Examples:
- one definition  
- one clause  
- one boundary line  
- one relationship block  

Avoid big motions.  
Avoid sweeping changes.

---

## 5. Step 4 — Add One Gentle Stroke
Write one small, clear, non-coercive line.

Examples:
- “This component may not override X.”  
- “This relationship must remain non-collapsing.”  
- “This rule preserves pacing.”  

One stroke.  
Not ten.

---

## 6. Step 5 — Step Back and Look
Pause.  
Ask softly:

“Does this feel stable?”

If yes — you’re done.  
If not — add one more tiny stroke.

---

## 7. Step 6 — Stop Early
Bob Ross rule:  
Stop before you think you’re finished.

Leave room for future joy.

---

## 8. Step 7 — Commit the Change
Save the file.  
Commit the update.  
Let the canvas dry.

---

## 9. Step 8 — Return to Stillness
Stillness is part of the workflow.  
It keeps your project gentle and non-collapsing.

---

## 10. Step 9 — Choose the Next Region
When you’re ready — not before — choose the next region.

The loop is gentle.  
Never forced.

---

## 11. Summary
This template helps anyone design a calm, ND-safe, Bob-Ross-style workflow for building their system.
