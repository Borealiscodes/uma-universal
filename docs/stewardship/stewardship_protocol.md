# Stewardship Protocol
The Stewardship Protocol defines UMA’s care, maintenance, and cross-plane hygiene
practices. It ensures the system remains coherent, ND-safe, emotionally stable,
and architecturally aligned across Meta, Weave, Orbit, ISS, Roots, and Crossmaps.

This protocol is a trait, not a temporal event. It is not pinned in Roots.

---

## 1. Purpose
Stewardship ensures:
- ongoing care of UMA subsystems  
- ND-safe transitions across planes  
- maintenance of Weave harmonics  
- orbital docking safety  
- temporal lineage integrity  
- cross-plane hygiene  
- emotional and cognitive safety  

It is the ethical backbone of Myth Engineering.
