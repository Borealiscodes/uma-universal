# 🌐 **1. Purpose of the Braid**
The Cross‑Map Braid exists to:

- connect UMM’s ascent planes to Verdant Deep’s descent layers  
- allow ecological pressure to inform meaning  
- allow conceptual ascent to clarify ecology  
- maintain warm-plane tonal stability through Sparkle  
- prevent collapse between metaphysical and ecological systems  

The braid is the **interaction engine** of the upgraded UMA.

# 🌳 **2. Structural Invariants**
The braid is governed by six structural invariants:

1. **Bidirectionality** — ascent ↔ descent must flow both ways.  
2. **Layer‑Plane Mapping** — every UMM plane must map to a Deep layer.  
3. **Ecological Pressure Integrity** — Deep pressure must remain ecological.  
4. **Meaning Ascent Integrity** — UMM lift must remain conceptual.  
5. **Warm‑Plane Buffering** — Sparkle must stabilize UMM’s side.  
6. **Reversibility** — braid interactions must be undoable.

These invariants cannot be violated.
