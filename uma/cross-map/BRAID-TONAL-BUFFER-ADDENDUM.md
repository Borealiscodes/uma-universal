The Tonal Buffer exists to:

- stabilize UMM’s witness and meaning planes  
- prevent ecological overload during descent  
- prevent conceptual collapse during ascent  
- maintain ND‑safe transitions  
- preserve warm‑plane tone across the braid  

Sparkle is the **only** tonal buffer permitted in the braid.

- UMM conceptual scaffolds  
- braid interaction zones

- Verdant Deep layers  
- ecological mechanics
