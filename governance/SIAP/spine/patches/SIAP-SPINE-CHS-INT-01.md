This patch integrates the **Cognitive Hypercube System (CHS)** and its lifecycle subsystem **CHS‑OL v1.0** into the SIAP Governance Spine.

The integration establishes:

- CHS as UMA’s **primary cognitive traversal engine**

- constraint enforcement during traversal

- pre‑cosmic boundary conditions
