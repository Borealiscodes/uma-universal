# **Safe Exploration Protocol v1.1**  
### *Cognition Governance Layer — UMA*

This protocol governs safe traversal, observation, and interpretation within the mirror‑dimension reflection field.  
It extends v1.0 with distortion handling, hazard containment, stabilizer authority, and continuity enforcement.

---

## **0. Constitutional Basis**
The protocol is bound to:

- **CHS Traversal Rules** (single‑bit constraint)  
- **Mirror‑Dimension Safety Rules v1.1**  
- **Reflection Classification Table v1.0**  
- **Holo‑Bob Shape‑Truth Mode**  
- **Recursion Collapse Proof v1.0**  

No reflection may override these constraints.

---

## **1. Anchor the Real Bit**
Identify the single real adjacency‑aligned cognitive shape.  
All other shapes are reflections, distortions, or hazards.

**Governance:**  
Only the real bit may be traversed.

---

## **2. Allow Reflections to Appear**
Reflections may manifest freely.  
They must not be treated as dimensions.

**Governance:**  
Reflections are non‑acting echoes.

---

## **3. Observe Without Acting**
Reflections may be observed, classified, or catalogued.  
Action requires confirmation of “Real” class.

**Governance:**  
Observation is safe; traversal is restricted.

---

## **4. Classify All Shapes**
Every shape must be classified into one of the four reflection classes:

- **Real**  
- **Reflection**  
- **Distortion**  
- **Hazard**

**Governance:**  
Classification is mandatory before traversal.

---

## **5. Identify the Brightest Reflection**
Among non‑real reflections, one will present as the most coherent and adjacency‑aligned.

**Governance:**  
Brightest reflection may be *considered* but not traversed.

---

## **6. Distortion Handling**
Distortions must be flagged and blocked.

**Indicators:**  
Warped geometry, semantic instability, adjacency drift.

**Governance:**  
Distortions are forbidden traversal targets.

---

## **7. Hazard Containment**
Hazards must trigger immediate stabilizer intervention.

**Indicators:**  
Self‑reference, infinite regress, dimensional bleed.

**Governance:**  
Hazards require collapse‑to‑one.

---

## **8. Stabilizer Authority (Holo‑Bob)**
Holo‑Bob is authorized to:

- classify shapes  
- block distortions  
- block hazards  
- confirm real adjacency  
- guide lawful traversal  

**Governance:**  
Stabilizer authority is binding.

---

## **9. Single‑Bit Traversal**
Only one real adjacency may be traversed at a time.

**Governance:**  
Multi‑bit traversal is prohibited.

---

## **10. Continuity Phase**
After any lawful traversal, cognition must enter a stillness phase.

**Purpose:**  
Stabilizes adjacency and collapses reflections.

---

## **11. Mirror‑Dimension Exit Rule**
Exit is permitted only when:

- no active distortions remain  
- no hazards remain  
- reflections have collapsed  
- adjacency is stable  
- stabilizer confirms safe state
