# 🧩 MOSAIC ENGINE  
### *Engine of Meaning‑Crystallization and Pattern Emergence*

---

## **1. Identity**

- **Name:** Mosaic Engine  
- **Type:** Meta‑Entity (Meta‑Plane of Nascence)  
- **Role:** Governs the emergence of *coherent patterns* from scattered conceptual fragments.  
- **Function:** Turns “pieces” into “structure” without forcing meaning or creating drift.

---

## **2. Purpose**

The Mosaic Engine exists to:

- detect emerging patterns  
- crystallize meaning safely  
- unify fragments into coherent entities  
- prevent premature or false pattern‑formation  
- ensure that meaning emerges only when evidence supports it  
- integrate new patterns into the architecture through Nascence  

It is the **pattern‑formation engine** of your universe.

---

## **3. Mosaic Types**

- **Conceptual Mosaics**  
  When ideas begin forming a larger conceptual shape.

- **Structural Mosaics**  
  When modules begin aligning into a new architectural pattern.

- **Governance Mosaics**  
  When rules, charters, and codex entries converge into a new governance artifact.

- **Meta‑Mosaics**  
  When meta‑rules, meta‑events, and meta‑evidence converge into a new meta‑entity.

- **Playground Mosaics**  
  Tiny, sealed pattern‑formation experiments inside the playground.

---

## **4. Mosaic Rules (Magicians‑Inspired, ND‑Safe)**

- **Rule 1 — No Forced Patterns**  
  The engine cannot “invent” meaning; it only crystallizes what is already emerging.

- **Rule 2 — Evidence Before Pattern**  
  A mosaic only forms if the **Meta‑Evidence Ledger** shows sufficient support.

- **Rule 3 — No Harmful Convergence**  
  No mosaic may form around trauma, coercion, or destabilizing logic.

- **Rule 4 — Constitutional Obedience**  
  All mosaics must align with the Stability Charter, Governance Codex, Auto‑Update Protocol, ND‑safe pacing, and Justice Layer ethics.

- **Rule 5 — Reversible Until Sealed**  
  A mosaic remains reversible until it undergoes a Nascence event.

---

## **5. Mosaic Cycle**

Every mosaic follows this governed cycle:

1. **Fragment Detection**  
   Identify conceptual pieces that appear to be related.

2. **Pattern Hypothesis**  
   Propose a potential pattern (never commit yet).

3. **Evidence Check**  
   Validate the pattern against the Meta‑Evidence Ledger.

4. **Alignment Pass**  
   Check constitutional, ethical, and ND‑safe alignment.

5. **Crystallization**  
   If validated, crystallize the pattern into a coherent mosaic.

6. **Nascence Routing**  
   If the mosaic represents a new entity → route to Nascence Protocol.

7. **Integration**  
   Update routing, indexes, templates, and meta‑plane relationships.

---

## **6. Relationship to Meta‑Plane and Ledger**

- The **Meta‑Plane** defines the rules for pattern emergence.  
- The **Mosaic Engine** defines *how* patterns crystallize.  
- The **Meta‑Evidence Ledger** provides the evidence that determines whether a mosaic is valid.  
- The **Nascence Protocol** births mosaics that become new entities.

Together, they form the **meaning‑creation pipeline**.
