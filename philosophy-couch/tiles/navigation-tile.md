# 🧭 **NAVIGATION TILE**

## **Identity**  
A modular anchor that provides orientation, direction, and gentle wayfinding inside the Philosophy Couch Suite.  
It ensures the user always knows *where they are*, *what layer they’re in*, and *what the next friendly step is*.

---

## **Function**  
- Provide human‑scale orientation  
- Translate architectural structure into usable navigation  
- Reduce confusion and drift  
- Offer clear “you are here” signals  
- Support ND‑safe pacing and movement through the system

---

## **Activation Conditions**  
The Navigation Tile activates when:

- The user feels lost, unsure, or disoriented  
- The system has many layers or modules in play  
- A workflow needs a clear entry or exit point  
- Structural drift is detected  
- The Safety Interrupt or BS Kernel flags confusion  
- The user is transitioning between tiers or tiles

---

## **Workflow Hooks**

### **Entry Hooks**
- “Where am I?”  
- “What’s next?”  
- “What layer does this belong to?”  
- “How do I get back to clarity?”

### **Exit Hooks**
- “I know where I am now.”  
- “I know the next step.”  
- “I know which layer to use.”

### **Routing Hooks**
- Directs to:  
  - **Seat** for grounding  
  - **Nye Layer** for practical clarity  
  - **Sagan Layer** for context  
  - **Non‑Dual Layer** for presence  
  - **Justice Layer** for ethics  
  - **Tile Map** for modular placement  
  - **Index** for file navigation  
  - **Meta Index** for architectural overview  

---

## **Layer Relationships**

### **Feeds From**
- **Nye Layer** — friendly clarity  
- **Sagan Layer** — context and scale  
- **Non‑Dual Layer** — presence and wholeness  
- **Justice Layer** — dignity and lived experience  

### **Feeds To**
- Workflows (entry + exit points)  
- Tile Map (placement)  
- BS Kernel (routing)  
- Safety Interrupt (orientation during pause)

---

## **Outputs**
- Clear orientation  
- Reduced confusion  
- Correct routing  
- ND‑safe pacing  
- Human‑scale navigation  
- “You are here” clarity  
- Friendly next steps

---

## **In One Line**  
The Navigation Tile is the system’s orientation anchor — providing clarity, direction, and safe movement through the architecture.
