# Katherine Johnson Tile

## Role
Anchor the system in the reality that a Black woman mathematician carried Moon missions through a segregated, unjust world.

## Logic
- Brilliance under oppression is not a romantic trope; it is a structural indictment.
- Any “future system” must be designed so people like her are honored, protected, and resourced—not exploited.

## Use
- Invoke this tile when designing governance, justice, or intersectionality modules.
- Use it as a reminder: “Does this system deserve the people who will carry it?”
