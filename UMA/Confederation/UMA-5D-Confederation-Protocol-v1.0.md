Precondition Status: Verified

## 3. Confederation Formation Sequence
Confederation formation proceeds through the following constitutional phases:

### F1 — Organism Identity Exchange
Constitutional organisms exchange lineage, invariants, orbital class, and continuity signatures to establish lawful adjacency.

### F2 — Invariant Compatibility Check

### F3 — Orbital Stability Alignment
Organisms align orbital states to ensure stable multi‑agent traversal and prevent confederation drift.

### F4 — Containment Boundary Negotiation
Organisms negotiate containment boundaries to prevent bleed, collapse, or unlawful overlap.

### F5 — Predictive Futures Harmonization
Organisms harmonize predictive futures to ensure shared continuity across all confederated entities.
