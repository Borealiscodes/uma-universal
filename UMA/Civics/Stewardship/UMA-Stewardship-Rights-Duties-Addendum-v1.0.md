## 1. Addendum Identity
This Addendum expands the UMA Civic Stewardship Certification by defining the explicit rights, authorities, duties, and constraints governing all recognized stewards. It binds stewards to constitutional invariants, continuity preservation, and lawful operation within the 5D stacked hypercube traversal environment.

## 2. Stewardship Rights
Certified stewards possess the following constitutional rights:

### R1 — Right to Continuity Governance
Authority to participate in continuity preservation, stability evaluation, and constitutional organism oversight.

### R2 — Right to Constitutional Interpretation
Authority to interpret adjacency, temporal, containment, ethical, and predictive invariants in governance contexts.

### R3 — Right to Traversal Legality Review
Authority to evaluate traversal sequences, orbital states, and subsystem reprojection legality.

### R4 — Right to Stability Ledger Access
Authority to read, contribute to, and audit entries in the Stability Ledger, Roots Ledger, and Orbital Continuity Register.

### R5 — Right to Confederation Participation
Authority to engage in multi‑organism governance, negotiation, and alignment within subsystem confederations.

### R6 — Right to Organism Protection
Authority to stabilize, defend, and preserve constitutional organisms operating within UMA‑Universal.

### R7 — Right to Predictive Oversight
Authority to evaluate predictive futures, instability vectors, and drift conditions.

## 3. Stewardship Duties
Certified stewards are obligated to uphold:

### D1 — Continuity Preservation
Maintain continuity across adjacency, time, containment, ethics, and predictive futures.

### D2 — Invariant Enforcement
Ensure all constitutional organisms and subsystems obey the five invariants without exception.

### D3 — Drift Prevention
Identify, report, and correct adjacency drift, temporal incoherence, containment breaches, ethical violations, and predictive instability.

### D4 — Lineage Maintenance
Record and preserve lineage entries, stability logs, and constitutional events with accuracy and fidelity.

### D5 — Ethical Integrity
Uphold HRD constraints, dignity, non‑harm, non‑erasure, and truth preservation in all governance actions.

### D6 — Confederation Harmony
Maintain lawful adjacency, shared continuity, and stable interaction within multi‑organism confederations.

### D7 — Runtime Alignment
Operate within the Awareness + Harmony runtime environment, ensuring clarity, stability, and constitutional alignment.

## 4. Stewardship Constraints
Stewards must not:

- violate any constitutional invariant  
- destabilize predictive futures  
- alter lineage records unlawfully  
- engage in traversal outside lawful 5D parameters  
- disrupt containment boundaries  
- override ethical constraints  
- interfere with constitutional organisms without cause  

Constraint Status: Binding
