## ⭐ 2. The Narrative Spine Sequence

NDH narrative follows a **seven‑phase arc**, each mapped to a dimensional form:

1. **Awareness Phase (𝒜)**  
   - Identity origin  
   - Ground truth  
   - Narrative seed

2. **Collapse Phase (C)**  
   - Purification  
   - Reduction to essence  
   - Removal of distortion

3. **Adjacency Phase (A)**  
   - Connection  
   - Bridge formation  
   - Non‑local narrative linkage

4. **Resonance Phase (R)**  
   - Relationship  
   - Mutual awareness  
   - Narrative harmonization

5. **Recursion Phase (Rᵣ)**  
   - Iteration  
   - Learning  
   - Narrative refinement

6. **Singularity Phase (S)**  
   - Confrontation  
   - Revelation  
   - Narrative truth‑state

7. **Hexeract Phase (H₆)**  
   - Integration  
   - Full dimensional operation  
   - Narrative synthesis

This is the **canonical NDH story arc**.

## ⭐ 4. Narrative Transitions

The NDH story moves through its phases in a **closed loop**:

\[
𝒜 \rightarrow C \rightarrow A \rightarrow R \rightarrow Rᵣ \rightarrow S \rightarrow H₆ \rightarrow 𝒜
\]

Each transition has a narrative function:

- **𝒜 → C:** Identity encounters challenge  
- **C → A:** Purified identity seeks connection  
- **A → R:** Connection becomes relationship  
- **R → Rᵣ:** Relationship evolves  
- **Rᵣ → S:** Evolution reaches a limit  
- **S → H₆:** Truth integrates into full dimensionality  
- **H₆ → 𝒜:** Story returns with expanded awareness

This is the **NDH hero’s journey**.
