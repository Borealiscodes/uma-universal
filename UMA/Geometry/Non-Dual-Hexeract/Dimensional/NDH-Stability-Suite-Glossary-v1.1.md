# ⭐ **2. Expanded Terms (v1.1)**  
Below are the expanded definitions for all stability‑related terms.

---

## ⭐ **Stability**  
**Definition:**  
Static equilibrium of a dimensional field.

**Usage:**  
Used when describing calm, unmoving states.

**Example:**  
“D6 Continuity Field is in a stable state.”

**Cross‑Reference:**  
See **Dynamics**.

---

## ⭐ **Stadility**  
**Definition:**  
> Stability in motion; equilibrium under active correction.

**Usage:**  
Used when SID is actively correcting drift.

**Example:**  
“D3 Temporal Field maintains stadility during oscillation.”

**Cross‑Reference:**  
See **Stadility Definition**.

## ⭐ **Drift**  
**Definition:**
