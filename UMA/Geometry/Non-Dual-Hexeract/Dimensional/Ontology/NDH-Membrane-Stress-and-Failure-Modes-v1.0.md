# **II. Stress Types in NDH Membranes**

NDH membranes experience **six stress types**, each purely conceptual:

### **1. Ontological Stress**
Pressure from existence or anti‑existence.

### **2. Dimensional Stress**
Pressure from extension or contraction.

### **3. Curvature Stress**
Pressure from Universe or Anti‑Universe curvature.

### **4. Continuity Stress**
Pressure from forward or reverse time.

### **5. Dynamic Stress**
Pressure from motion or anti‑motion.

### **6. Interaction Stress**
Pressure from attempted cross‑stack influence.

Each stress type is **non‑physical** and **non‑harmful**, but mechanically meaningful.

# **IV. Stress Curves (Dimensional Mechanics)**

NDH membranes follow **non‑linear stress curves**:

### **Positive Stack Stress Curve**
\[
\sigma_{+}(x) = x^2
\]

### **Negative Stack Stress Curve**
\[
\sigma_{-}(x) = -x^2
\]

### **Interpretation**
Positive stress increases quadratically with extension.  
Negative stress increases quadratically with contraction.

Membranes must resist both simultaneously.

# **V. Failure Modes (Governed & Safe)**

NDH membranes cannot fail destructively.  
They fail in **governed, non‑harmful, non‑propagating modes**.

### **1. AEM Failure Mode: Null‑State Saturation**
The membrane becomes fully opaque.  
No interaction is possible.  
System remains stable.

### **2. NIS Failure Mode: Surface Thinning**
The anti‑surface becomes visually thin.  
Interaction remains impossible.

### **3. DPM Failure Mode: Dimensional Bleed**
PF and NF fields appear visually closer.  
No adjacency occurs.

### **4. SSM Failure Mode: Narrative Shimmer**
Stability and Stadility narratives flicker.  
No cross‑governance influence occurs.

### **5. UAM Failure Mode: Curvature Ripple**
Universe and Anti‑Universe curvature oscillates.  
No curvature inversion occurs.

### **6. SM Failure Mode: Zero‑State Echo**
Singularity traversal produces a faint echo.  
No singularity collapse occurs.

All failure modes are **safe**, **bounded**, and **non‑propagating**.

# **VII. Membrane Stress Propagation Rules**

Stress cannot propagate across membranes.

### **Rule 1: No Cross‑Spine Stress**
Positive Spine stress cannot reach Negative Spine.  
Negative Spine stress cannot reach Positive Spine.

### **Rule 2: No Cross‑Field Stress**
PF stress cannot reach NF.  
NF stress cannot reach PF.

### **Rule 3: No Cross‑Narrative Stress**
Stability narrative stress cannot reach Stadility.  
Stadility narrative stress cannot reach Stability.

### **Rule 4: No Cross‑Curvature Stress**
Universe curvature stress cannot reach Anti‑Universe.  
Anti‑Universe curvature stress cannot reach Universe.

These rules prevent **dimensional drift**.
