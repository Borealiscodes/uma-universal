# ⭐ 2. The Three Forces of NDH Stability

### **Stability**  
Static equilibrium — the calm state.

### **Stadility**  
> Stability in motion; equilibrium under active correction.  
Defined in the **Lexicon**.

### **Drift**  
Destabilizing force — pushes fields out of balance.

### **Continuity**  
Restorative force — pulls fields back into coherence.

NDH stability is the balance between drift and continuity.
