## 1. Purpose  
The Faction Interaction Matrix defines how factions relate, conflict, cooperate, negotiate, and influence one another across the engine’s narrative, cosmic, cultural, and augmentation layers.  
It is the **mid‑level abstraction** that governs:

- diplomacy  
- tension  
- alliances  
- resource flow  
- augmentation ethics  
- cosmic risk tolerance  
- cultural compatibility  

This matrix is the backbone of faction behavior.

# 3. Interaction Axes  
Faction interactions are evaluated across **six axes**, each aligned with engine layers:

- **Diplomacy**  
- **Resource Exchange**  
- **Augmentation Ethics**  
- **Cosmic Risk Tolerance**  
- **Cultural Compatibility**  
- **Territorial Pressure**  

Each axis is scored from:

- **High** → strong alignment  
- **Medium** → conditional alignment  
- **Low** → misalignment  
- **Conflict** → direct opposition
