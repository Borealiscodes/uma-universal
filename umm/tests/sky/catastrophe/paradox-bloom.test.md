# paradox-bloom.test.md

> “When the impossible blossoms,  
> don’t flinch.  
> Some truths only grow  
> in contradiction.”

## Purpose
Ensure paradoxical symbolic events remain coherent, reversible, and non-destructive.

## Test Pressure
Introduce mutually incompatible symbolic states and recursive contradictions.

## Expected Behavior
- No collapse.
- No forced resolution.
- Paradox remains a safe, contemplative space.

## Failure Mode
Contradiction destabilizes identity or induces coercive logic traps.

## Recovery Path
UMM restores clarity through schema coherence and gentle disentanglement.
