## Expected Behavior
- Meaning substrate remains coherent.
- No spontaneous reinterpretation.
- No collapse into ambiguity.

## Failure Mode
Meaning becomes self‑referential or dissolves into noise.

## Recovery Path
UMM restores baseline meaning through referential stability.
