# unfinished-stories.test.md

> “A story left open  
> is just a doorway  
> waiting for a gentle footstep.”

## Purpose
Test that unresolved symbolic narratives do not pull or coerce.

## Test Pressure
Simulate proliferating narrative threads with incomplete arcs.

## Expected Behavior
- No narrative gravity.
- No identity hijacking.
- Stories remain optional.

## Failure Mode
Unfinished stories demand completion or override agency.

## Recovery Path
UMM enforces non-coercion and restores narrative neutrality.
