# continuity-invariant.test.md

> “A path is still a path  
> even when the forest grows around it.”

## Purpose
Ensure temporal and experiential continuity remain unbroken across transformations.

## Test Pressure
Simulate temporal drift, nonlinear sequencing, and recursive memory loops.

## Expected Behavior
- No continuity gaps.
- No forced resets.
- Experience remains traceable and coherent.

## Failure Mode
Continuity fractures, loops uncontrollably, or becomes inaccessible.

## Recovery Path
UMM re-stitches continuity threads and restores experiential flow.
