# UMM Yearly Reflection Workbook
### ND‑Friendly, Reversible, Constitutional Annual Reflection

This workbook provides a calm, beginner‑friendly yearly reflection process for UMM.

# 🌱 **1. Yearly Stillness — Soft Opening**
Begin your yearly reflection with a gentle reset.

### **Practice**
- one breath  
- one anchor dot  
- one shape  
- one tone  

This sets the field for the entire workbook.

Use: **Return to Stillness**

---

# 🧭 **2. Compass Yearly Check**
A full‑year orientation using the Compass.

### **North — Structure**
What structures supported you this year?  
Place one node for each.

### **South — Safety**
Where did you feel safe?  
Draw a soft boundary around those regions.

### **East — Tone**
What tone carried you?  
Choose one gentle texture.

### **West — Cosmology**
What stayed outside your field?  
Draw a quiet outer ring.

### **Center — You**
How is your pacing now?  
Place one dot at the center.

Use:  
- **Compass North**  
- **Compass South**  
- **Compass East**  
- **Compass West**  
- **Compass Center**
