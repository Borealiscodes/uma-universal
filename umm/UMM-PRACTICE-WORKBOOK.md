# 🌱 **1. Warm‑Up Practice — Settling the Field**
These exercises help you begin gently.

### **Exercise A — One Breath**
Take one slow breath.  
Let the field soften.

### **Exercise B — One Anchor**
Place a tiny dot in the center of your map.

### **Exercise C — One Shape**
Draw a simple shape (circle, triangle, square).

### **Exercise D — One Tone**
Choose a gentle tone for the field.

Use: **Return to Stillness**

---

# 🧭 **2. Compass Practice — Orientation Skills**
Practice using each direction of the Compass.

### **Exercise A — North (Structure)**
Draw a small circle marking “where I am.”

### **Exercise B — South (Safety)**
Add a soft boundary around your map.

### **Exercise C — East (Tone)**
Choose a gentle texture or color.

### **Exercise D — West (Cosmology)**
Draw a quiet outer ring to keep cosmology outside.

### **Exercise E — Center (You)**
Pause and ask:  
**“Is this still gentle for me?”**

Use:  
- **Compass North**  
- **Compass South**  
- **Compass East**  
- **Compass West**  
- **Compass Center**
