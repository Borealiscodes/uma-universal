# **8. Microcard Safety Rules**

Microcards must **never** include:

- intensity  
- urgency  
- emotional promises  
- dependency cues  
- identity claims  
- directive behavior

- softness  
- reversibility  
- symbolic framing  
- ND‑friendly pacing  
- gentle tone
