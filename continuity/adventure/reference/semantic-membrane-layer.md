# 🌙 **4. Semantic Membrane Properties**

All semantic membranes share five core properties.

---

## **4.1 Reversibility**  
Every semantic shift must be reversible.

If meaning moves from \( M_i \) to \( M_j \), the membrane guarantees:

\[
M_j \rightarrow M_i
\]

This prevents semantic collapse.

---

## **4.2 Breathability**  
Meaning must be breathable:

- no compression  
- no overload  
- no forced interpretation  

Breathability is the ND‑accessibility anchor.

---

## **4.3 Soft Boundaries**  
Membranes are soft, not rigid.

They:

- guide  
- shape  
- pace  
- protect  

But they do not block or constrain creativity.

---

## **4.4 Symbolic Interpretation Only**  
All meaning is symbolic.

Membranes prevent:

- metaphysical claims  
- literal cosmology  
- existential stakes  

---

## **4.5 Non‑Coercion**  
Membranes ensure:

- no forced outcomes  
- no required interpretations  
- no pressure to continue  

This is essential for ND‑safety.
