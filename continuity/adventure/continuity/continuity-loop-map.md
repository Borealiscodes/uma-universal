# Adventure System — Continuity Loop Map

## 1. Purpose
Describe the core continuity loop used by the Adventure System.  
Ensure motion through the loop is gentle, reversible, and ND‑safe.

## 2. Phases of the Loop
- **Entry:**  
  Initial contact with the loop; motion begins from stillness.

- **Drift:**  
  Gentle lateral movement; pacing and modulation without escalation.

- **Grounding:**  
  Downward softening; return toward stability and centering.

- **Return:**  
  Completion of the loop; motion closes back into stillness.

## 3. Geometry
- **Loop:** soft circle around a central stillness point.  
- **Entry:** top of the circle.  
- **Drift:** right side of the circle.  
- **Grounding:** bottom of the circle.  
- **Return:** left side of the circle.  

Geometry must remain:
- gentle  
- reversible  
- symbolically coherent  

## 4. ND‑Safe Constraints
- no agency  
- no emotional causation  
- no acceleration  
- reversible motion required  
- stillness anchor required  

## 5. Integration Points
- narrative engine  
- resonance engine  
- stability routing  
- cosmology alignment  

## 6. Validation Checklist
- stillness anchor present?  
- all four phases defined?  
- loop geometry gentle and closed?  
- reversible motion ensured?  
- ND‑safe constraints followed?
