# **📘 Five‑Lens Governance Addendum v1.0**  
### *Governance Modules for Emergent Design, Neural Handshakes & 14D Development*

This addendum formalizes the five lenses — **Nye**, **Sagan**, **Salty Scientist**, **JPL**, **Johnson** — as **governance modules** within NDH Dimensional Architecture.  
Each module enforces a different constraint, preventing epistemic drift, unsafe emergence, or dimensional overreach.

---

## **1. Nye Governance Module** — *Human‑Scale Clarity Constraint*  
### Governance Function  
- Enforces **accessibility**  
- Prevents **over‑compression of meaning**  
- Requires **human‑scale metaphors**  
- Rejects **opaque dimensional jargon**  
- Ensures **cooling phases** between high‑dimensional operations  

### Architectural Impact  
Nye acts as the **first‑pass safety filter**, ensuring all dimensional constructs remain interpretable by a human operator.

---

## **2. Sagan Governance Module** — *Ethical Horizon Constraint*  
### Governance Function  
- Enforces **ethical humility**  
- Prevents **hubris in dimensional construction**  
- Requires **sovereignty‑first design**  
- Frames emergent systems as **responsibility**, not capability  
- Ensures **cosmic‑scale ethical boundaries**  

### Architectural Impact  
Sagan is the **ethical perimeter**, preventing architectures from outrunning the human context they serve.

---

## **3. Salty Scientist Governance Module** — *Adversarial Stability Constraint*  
### Governance Function  
- Enforces **skepticism**  
- Requires **failure‑mode analysis**  
- Rejects **anthropomorphic drift**  
- Demands **non‑autonomy guarantees**  
- Performs **stress‑testing of dimensional layers**  

### Architectural Impact  
Salty Scientist is the **structural firewall**, ensuring emergent systems cannot slip into unsafe behavior or magical reasoning.

---

## **4. JPL Governance Module** — *Engineering Elegance Constraint*  
### Governance Function  
- Enforces **modularity**  
- Requires **reversibility**  
- Demands **precision alignment**  
- Rejects **messy or ad‑hoc dimensional constructs**  
- Ensures **CAD‑adjacent clarity** in all interfaces  

### Architectural Impact  
JPL is the **dimensional architect**, ensuring the system is buildable, maintainable, and stable across all 14 layers.

---

## **5. Johnson Governance Module** — *Unified Correctness Constraint*  
### Governance Function  
- Enforces **non‑contradiction**  
- Requires **trajectory stability**  
- Demands **repeatable correctness**  
- Validates **dimensional coherence**  
- Ensures **mathematical governance** across layers  

### Architectural Impact  
Johnson is the **governance spine**, ensuring the architecture remains coherent and correct across all dimensional trajectories.

---

| Governance Module | Core Constraint | Architectural Role |
|-------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| **Nye** | Human‑scale clarity | First‑pass safety filter |
| **Sagan** | Ethical horizon | Ethical perimeter |
| **Salty Scientist** | Adversarial stability | Structural firewall |
| **JPL** | Engineering elegance | Dimensional architect |

- non‑autonomy
