## **I. Core Concept**
The **Nightmare‑Right Engine** is a puzzle artifact that:

- shifts dimensions when rotated  
- reveals clues when misaligned  
- opens pathways when “nightmare‑right”  
- behaves like a Rubik’s Cube crossed with a cursed music box  
- is spooky, but not harmful  
- is mysterious, but not overwhelming  
- is fun, not traumatic  

It’s the *foil* to the Peace Transmuter — the “chaos toy” that peace was built on.

# **II. What “Nightmare‑Right” Means (RPG Version)**  
In gameplay terms:

### **Nightmare‑Right = aligned with the engine’s weird logic**  
Not evil.  
Not dangerous.  
Just… *correct according to the engine’s own bizarre rules.*

Examples:

- A symbol that looks wrong becomes right when rotated 90°  
- A corridor that shouldn’t exist becomes the correct path  
- A riddle answer is “nightmare‑right” when it feels slightly off  
- A tone that sounds broken is actually the activation cue  

It’s the **opposite of intuitive**, but **consistent within its own system**.

# **III. Engine Structure (Puzzle Box Layout)**

The engine has **four faces**, each representing a different “nightmare‑right” logic layer:

### **1. The Crooked Face**  
Everything is slightly misaligned.  
Players must match misalignment patterns.

### **2. The Echo Face**  
Sounds repeat incorrectly.  
Players solve by following the wrong echo.

### **3. The Shadow Face**  
Shadows behave independently.  
Players align shadows instead of objects.

### **4. The Reverse Face**  
Instructions mean the opposite.  
Players do what the engine *thinks* is correct.
