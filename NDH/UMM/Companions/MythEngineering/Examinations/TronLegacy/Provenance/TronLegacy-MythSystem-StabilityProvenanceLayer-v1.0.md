## **1. Purpose**

The Myth‑System Stability ProvenanceLayer defines **how stability behaviors relate structurally** across the entire Tron Legacy myth‑system.  
It maps:

- stability relations between symbolic, physics, sovereignty, and provenance layers  
- dimensional constraints (7D–14D) on those relations  
- ND‑safe lineage of system‑level stability patterns  
- a governed, acyclic provenance graph for myth‑system stability  

This is **provenance of stability**, not provenance of story or identity.
