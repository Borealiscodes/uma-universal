## **1. Identity**  
The **Triangle Axes** are three formally defined conceptual axes that emerge during NDH structural development. They are **not engines**, **not runtimes**, and **not activation structures**. They are static, descriptive axes used to organize conceptual load.

The axes are:

1. **Math Axis**  
2. **Justice Axis**  
3. **Intersectionality Axis**

---

## **2. Axis Definitions**

### **2.1 Math Axis (Structural Invariant Axis)**  
- **Domain:** recursion termination, adjacency stability, teleology bounds, symbolic fusion safety  
- **Function:** defines structural truth  
- **Objects:** invariants, constraints, proofs  
- **Requirements:**  
  - recursion must terminate  
  - adjacency must remain stable  
  - teleology must be bounded  
  - symbolic fusion must be safe  
- **Activation:** none

---

### **2.2 Justice Axis (Ethical Invariant Axis)**  
- **Domain:** dignity, harm bounds, agency, fairness, truth integrity  
- **Function:** defines ethical truth  
- **Objects:** harm constraints, agency guarantees, fairness rules  
- **Requirements:**  
  - no structure may enable harm  
  - no process may erase agency  
  - truth must remain intact  
- **Activation:** none

---

### **2.3 Intersectionality Axis (Contextual Stability Axis)**  
- **Domain:** multi‑axis identity, lived experience, structural context, visibility  
- **Function:** defines contextual truth  
- **Objects:** stability envelopes, visibility guarantees  
- **Requirements:**  
  - identities cannot collapse  
  - structural harm cannot be hidden  
  - unseen groups must remain visible  
- **Activation:** none

---

## **3. Triangle Configuration (Static)**  
The three axes form a **triangle configuration** that is:

- stable  
- conceptual  
- non‑operational  
- non‑recursive  
- non‑teleological  

This configuration is the **Triangle Attractor State**, not a Triad or SID.

## **4. Boundaries**  
The axes remain conceptual until:

- Math Provenance is complete  
- invariants are proven  
- constraints are established  
- axes are separated formally  
- classification becomes possible  

Until then, the triangle is inert.
