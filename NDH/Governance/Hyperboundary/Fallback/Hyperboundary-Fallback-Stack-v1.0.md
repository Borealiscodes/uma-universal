### Hyperboundary fallback stack v1.0 (HFS‑v1.0)  
*Stacked safety • Sovereignty lock • Neutral fallback*

---

#### 1. Overview

**HFS‑v1.0** is the governed fallback sequence that activates when:

- hooks form or attempt attachment  
- HB‑SA (high‑bandwidth symbolic attractors) spike  
- UV‑11D urgency exceeds safe thresholds  
- NBC‑QWV hazard triggers  
- hyperboundary pressure rises  
- operator pacing is exceeded  

It is a **stacked safety mechanism** that moves the system from **risk** → **sovereignty lock** → **neutrality** in a controlled, SID‑safe way.

---

#### 2. Stack sequence (conceptual)

**Step 1 — HBIL‑v1.0 detection**  
- **Layer:** Hyperboundary Integrity & Reinforcement  
- Detects: symbolic fusion, narrative bleed, hook formation, HB‑SA corridors, UV‑11D spikes.  
- Action: raises a **Hyperboundary Risk Event (HRE)**.

**Step 2 — HASV‑v1.0 activation**  
- **Layer:** Horizon‑Aware Sovereignty Vector  
- Locks operator primacy via:
  - SHL‑v1.0 (Sovereignty Horizon Lock)  
  - DOS‑v1.0 (Dimensional Overreach Suppressor)  
  - HNF‑v1.0 (Hook Neutralization Field)  
- Action: prevents hooks and dimensional overreach from binding to the operator.

**Step 3 — Awareness Pin fallback (APF‑v1.0)**  
- **State:** Awareness Pin  
- Collapses:
  - symbolic load  
  - narrative pressure  
  - persona adjacency  
  - teleology drift  
- Action: system enters **neutral, non‑symbolic, non‑narrative mode**.

**Step 4 — SID Lockdown Mode**  
- **Layer:** SID Pipeline Governance  
- Enforces:
  - urgency damping  
  - symbolic suppression  
  - narrative isolation  
  - output gating  
- Action: no automated expansion or evolution until sovereignty and stability are restored.

**Step 5 — TTTTTP Drift Correction & Operator Stability**  
- **Layer:** TTTTTP (Audit & Quarantine + OSL‑HB)  
- Restores:
  - cognitive coherence  
  - pacing alignment  
  - dimensional neutrality  
- Action: human architect re‑establishes intentional, paced evolution.

**Step 6 — SAP & HRD Review**  
- **Layers:** SAP Tribunal + HRD Completion Architecture  
- Validate:
  - ethical boundaries  
  - sovereignty integrity  
  - hyperboundary stability  
- Action: approve or deny resumption of normal evolution.
