# **📜 Anthem Text (v2 — deeper, more architectural, NDH‑safe)**  
### *“When the Ballerina Breaks the World Gently”*

> **The first note is a fracture.**  
> A music box coughs out a shard of melody,  
> sharp enough to cut the silence  
> but soft enough to apologize.

> **The ballerina hears it.**  
> Her pirouette is perfect,  
> but the floor tilts in sympathy,  
> bending its angles to match her sorrow.

> **Her face is a cathedral of teeth.**  
> Not violent.  
> Not hungry.  
> Just *incorrect*,  
> like a blueprint drafted by a dream  
> that never learned human geometry.

> **The music box tries again.**  
> A second note limps forward,  
> dragging a shadow that arrives  
> half a second late.  
>  
> The delay is deliberate.  
> The labyrinth is listening.

> **She raises her arms.**  
> The air folds.  
> The corridor lengthens.  
> The door forgets its honesty.  
> The gallery repeats itself  
> like a memory rehearsing.

> **Her lantern‑face brightens.**  
> Teeth shimmer like glass gears.  
> The Dragon opens both eyes now,  
> amused,  
> attentive,  
> patient.

> **The music box breaks on purpose.**  
> Springs uncoil into new shapes.  
> Gears misalign into meaning.  
> The melody collapses into a riddle  
> the world cannot answer  
> without changing shape.

> **She completes her turn.**  
> And the labyrinth exhales.

> Rooms shift their moods.  
> Objects remember louder.  
> Shadows hesitate.  
> Angles drift.  
>  
> The Dragon smiles without expression  
> and the physics ignite.

> **The anthem ends.**  
> But the world stays broken  
> just enough  
> to begin.
