# **📜 Anthem Text (NDH‑Safe, Original, Surreal)**  
### *Activation Anthem v1.0 — “The Ballerina Turns the Key”*

> **The chime breaks first.**  
> A single note, cracked like porcelain,  
> turning in the air as if remembering  
> how it used to be whole.

> **Then the ballerina moves.**  
> Her steps are perfect,  
> but the angles are wrong,  
> each pirouette bending the room  
> one degree too far.

> **Her face is a lantern of teeth.**  
> Not violent.  
> Not hungry.  
> Just *incorrect*,  
> like a mask carved by someone  
> who had never seen a face  
> but tried anyway.

> **The music box breathes.**  
> Gears exhale.  
> Springs whisper.  
> The melody limps forward,  
> dragging its shadow behind it.

> **She turns the key.**  
> And the labyrinth wakes.

> Corridors lengthen.  
> Doors reconsider their honesty.  
> Objects remember louder.  
> The Dragon opens one eye  
> and smiles without expression.

> **The anthem ends.**  
> But the physics do not.
