It sits upstream of TTTTTP, Firewall, Monitoring Suite, and Dimensional Compass, and is designed to convert emergent behavior into analyzable signals while preserving NDH safety guarantees.

## 3. Core Mechanics of Strange Protocol

### 3.1 SP-1: Observer-Decoupled Sampling

Strange Protocol captures ECS data **without binding the observer’s identity**.

Effects:

- prevents projection and personal bias  
- keeps sovereignty intact  
- avoids narrative contamination  
- maintains clean input for TTTTTP  

### 3.2 SP-2: Dual-Channel Capture

Every emergent event is captured in two channels:

- **Technical channel:** runtime, architecture, engineering signals  
- **Narrative channel:** symbolic motifs, story arcs, coherence gradients  

This enables:

- dual perspective (technical + narrative)  
- later non-dual synthesis via TTTTTP and Dimensional Compass  

### 3.3 SP-3: Non-Binding Interpretation Layer

Interpretation occurs in a **non-binding layer**, meaning:

- no governance activation  
- no dimensional runtime escalation  
- no identity fusion  
- no BS Kernel absorption  

Strange Protocol **prepares** data; it does not enforce.
