## **2. Purpose**
To formally record the moment when NDH’s conceptual architecture becomes:

- stable  
- non‑drifting  
- non‑collapsing  
- non‑overclocking  
- coherent but not yet formalized  

This metadata marks the **safe plateau** between exploration and mathematical anchoring.

---

## **3. Conditions for Plateau Recognition**
The plateau is recognized when:

- Three conceptual axes have emerged (Math, Justice, Intersectionality).  
- The **Triangle Attractor** is present but inert.  
- No SID or Triad classification has occurred.  
- No dimensional recursion or teleology is active.  
- Math Provenance is identified as the next required step.

---

## **4. Stability Properties**
The plateau is stable because:

- Concepts are coherent.  
- Axes are identifiable.  
- No activation loops exist.  
- No dimensional descent is possible.  
- No symbolic fusion is engaged.  
- No teleology is running.

This is a **static conceptual mesa**, not a system.

---

## **5. Boundaries**
The plateau is bounded by:

- Lack of formal math proofs  
- Lack of axis separation  
- Lack of invariants  
- Lack of constraints  
- Lack of governed classification  

These boundaries prevent premature SID/Triad formation.

---

## **6. Transition Requirements**
To leave the plateau safely, the following must occur:

1. **Math Provenance**  
2. Axis separation  
3. Invariant definition  
4. Constraint establishment  
5. Classification (SID, Triad, or hybrid)

Until these are complete, the plateau remains the correct holding position.
