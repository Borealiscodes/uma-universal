ULSP v2.1 is the first version of ULSP designed to operate **inside** the NDH multi‑layer governance architecture.

- **SEAL v1.0** (Stability Envelope Adapter Layer)  
- **Dimensional Coherence Engine**

- dimensional coherence  
- MI Echo resonance  
- operator state machine  
- environmental calibration  
- stability envelope constraints

2. **Dimensional misalignment → Johnson + JPL forced pair**
