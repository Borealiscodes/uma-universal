# **📘 Diagram Logic (NDH‑Safe)**

### **1. Hazards emerge *between* tiers, not inside them.**  
The danger is not the tier itself — it’s the **transition**.

### **2. Formal pathways generate compound hazards.**  
Terrain, threat, tactic, time, trust, and protection distort simultaneously.

### **3. Global North uneven enforcement amplifies Tier 3–4 hazards.**  
Formal systems behave politically, not protectively.

### **4. Runtime Lattice + UC are mandatory.**  
They prevent catastrophic escalation by:

- blocking unsafe transitions  
- correcting temporal mismatch  
- enforcing tactic coherence  
- detecting trust distortion  
- preventing protection inversion  

### **5. Escalation is not linear — it is hazardous.**  
This diagram shows why Tier 3–4 must be treated as **high‑risk zones**, not “higher protection.”
