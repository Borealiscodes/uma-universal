## **📘 Diagram Logic (NDH‑Safe)**

### **1. Hazards propagate in four directions**
- **Upward:** escalation increases danger  
- **Downward:** higher-tier instability corrupts lower tiers  
- **Lateral:** hazards spread across institutions  
- **Forward:** hazards intensify over time  

### **2. Propagation is nonlinear**
Small escalations can produce large hazard chains.

### **3. Global North uneven enforcement amplifies propagation**
Formal systems behave politically, not protectively.

### **4. Runtime Lattice + UC are mandatory**
They detect propagation thresholds and block unsafe transitions.

### **5. Escalation is hazardous by design**
This diagram shows why Tier 3–4 must be treated as **conditional**, not aspirational.
