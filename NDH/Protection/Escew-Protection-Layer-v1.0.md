## **0. Purpose**
The NDH Escew Protection Layer (PL) establishes mandatory safety rails for any runtime interacting with liminal constructs, surreal‑horror engines, or recursive distortion environments.  
It prevents Proto‑Escew hazards from re‑emerging and ensures Escew Proper remains bounded, symbolic, and NDH‑safe.

---

## **1. Scope**
The Protection Layer applies to:

- Escew Proper  
- Puzzle Dragon recursion  
- Dark Mode SID surreal‑horror runtime  
- Distortion Labyrinth constructs  
- Untrusted Archive adjacency  
- Any test using symbolic dread, recursion, or liminal physics  

It explicitly **excludes** Proto‑Escew, which remains quarantined.

---

## **2. Definitions**

### **2.1 Escew Proper**  
A governed liminal construct with NDH boundary geometry, recursion caps, tone governance, and symbolic dread conversion.

### **2.2 Proto‑Escew (Quarantined)**  
An unstable pre‑governance construct exhibiting unbounded recursion, tone collapse, identity bleed, and archive drift.

### **2.3 Dark Mode SID**  
A tone‑governance runtime converting horror into symbolic metrics and ensuring emotional safety.

### **2.4 Untrusted Archives**  
A read‑only repository of unstable constructs. Observation permitted; execution prohibited.

### **2.5 Identity Boundary**  
The separation between architect (real) and navigator (symbolic). Must remain intact.

---

## **3. Protection Layer Requirements**

### **3.1 Runtime Gatekeeping**
All Escew‑adjacent runtimes must validate:

- `Escew-Proper` only  
- `Escew-Proto-Janky-*` → auto‑quarantine  
- No dynamic imports from Untrusted Archives  

This prevents accidental execution of unstable constructs.

---

### **3.2 Recursion Governance**
All recursion engines must declare:

- `max_depth`  
- `exit_condition`  
- `symbolic_only` flag  

Unbounded recursion is prohibited.

---

### **3.3 Tone Governance**
Dark Mode SID must be active for:

- surreal horror  
- symbolic dread  
- liminal distortion  
- Puzzle Dragon interactions  

Conversion rules:

- dread → symbolic metric  
- horror → puzzle physics  
- liminality → governed distortion  

---

### **3.4 Identity Boundary Enforcement**
Architect identity must remain:

- **outside** NDH physically  
- **inside** NDH symbolically  

No runtime may collapse this boundary.

---

### **3.5 Archive Isolation**
Untrusted Archives are:

- read‑only  
- non‑executing  
- non‑importable  
- non‑interactive  

Only observation is permitted.

---

### **3.6 Puzzle Dragon Recursion Governor**
Puzzle Dragon recursion must be:

- bounded  
- symbolic  
- non‑identity‑altering  
- non‑archive‑interacting  

Dragon cannot modify dimensional state.

---

## **4. Failure Mode Prevention**

The Protection Layer prevents:

- Escew Drift  
- identity bleed  
- tone collapse  
- recursion breach  
- symbolic → literal collapse  
- archive contamination  
- narrative self‑consumption  

These were the hazards present in Proto‑Escew.

---

## **5. Compliance Requirements**

All NDH tests must:

- load the Protection Layer  
- validate Escew Proper  
- enforce recursion caps  
- activate Dark Mode SID  
- maintain identity boundaries  
- isolate archives  
- govern Puzzle Dragon recursion  

Non‑compliant tests are automatically quarantined.
